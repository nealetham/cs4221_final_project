                                                                    Maersk inaugurates first bonded warehouse in Vietnam

                                                                    Located strategically within the Hai Phong seaport area, Maersk's bonded warehouse serves as a crucial node in the region's international transport network.

                                                                    November 4, 2024
                                                                    Share
                                                                    • Copy Link
                                                                    • Share on X
                                                                    • Share on Linkedin
                                                                    • Share on Facebook

                                                                    Maersk has inaugurated its first own-licensed bonded warehouse in Vietnam, situated at SLP Park, Nam Dinh Vu Industrial Park in Hai Phong province.

                                                                    Maersk Contract Logistics operates the warehouse, which is set to bolster the company’s presence in Northern Vietnam amid growing import and export demands.

                                                                    The opening event was attended by Lasse Pedersen Hjortshøj, the Commercial Counsellor of the Danish Embassy in Vietnam, emphasising the commitment to international business expansion.

                                                                    Located strategically within the Hai Phong seaport area, Maersk’s bonded warehouse serves as a crucial node in the region’s international transport network.

                                                                    The facility’s integration with Maersk’s existing fulfilment centre in Hai Phong and the forthcoming terminal at Lach Huyen port, developed in partnership with APM Terminals and Hateco, is poised to enhance Maersk’s storage solutions and operational efficiency.

                                                                    This synergy is expected to streamline supply chains for customers and tap into local growth opportunities.

                                                                    Maersk Mekong Area Managing Director Kevin Burrell said: “Maersk always aims to bring the most optimal logistics solutions to our customers. We believe that running our own bonded warehouse directly helps us to synchronize processes for our clients.

                                                                    “It also improves service quality and cost controls. We sincerely thank Amazon for choosing Maersk as a long-term partner in Vietnam and look forward to achieving greater success together.”

                                                                    Customers utilising the warehouse will experience improved inventory management and the flexibility to move goods without accruing duties, thus optimising their supply chains and reducing costs. These benefits contribute to faster delivery times and adherence to customs regulations.

                                                                    The warehouse boasts state-of-the-art technology for efficient and secure operations. Features include a modern racking system, sorting processes, inventory management, and an automatic barcode system for high accuracy.

                                                                    Energy efficiency is a priority, with natural lighting from skylight panels, energy-saving LED lights, and motion sensors in offices and public spaces.

                                                                    Amazon Vietnam, a cross-border e-commerce service provider, will be the first customer to utilise the new facility. Maersk Contract Logistics will offer an array of services including bonded warehousing, domestic fulfilment, customs procedures, and inland transportation.

                                                                    Amazon GXD Origin Operation Senior Manager Chris Ji said: “We are thrilled to be celebrating the grand opening of this new warehouse facility here in Vietnam. Over the past 3 years, Maersk has embodied the principles of customer obsession and operational excellence, driving remarkable progress for our business. We look forward to the future milestones this facility will enable as we further strengthen our partnership.”

                                                                    Maersk is also partnering with industrial and logistics infrastructure developers, SLP.

                                                                    SLP CEO Edwin Chee said: “We are honored that Maersk has chosen us as their trusted partner in establishing the first own-licensed bonded warehouse in Northern Vietnam at SLP Park Hai Phong.

                                                                    “This partnership underscores our shared commitment to providing top-tier logistics solutions. Together, we look forward to driving growth and setting new benchmarks for the industry and the region’s development.”
