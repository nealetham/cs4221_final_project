The best Amazon Cyber Monday deals for 2022

By Noah McGraw Published November 29, 2022
This story is part of the Digital Trends Black Friday coverage 2024

Cyber Monday is in the rear view, but there are still a lot of strangler deals hanging on. If you haven’t checked everything off your shopping list, consider this your last call to snap up some of the amazing Cyber Monday deals below. We already saw a ton of Amazon Cyber Monday deals sell out fast, so if you like the look of any of the below deals, make sure to add them to your basket and check out now. With deals on everything from cheap smart home devices to expensive gaming laptops, how do you know where to start? We’ve rounded up the best of the best right here, but remember — this is the last major sales event of the year, so to get your goodies in time for the holidays, make sure you order today.

Echo Dot (3rd Gen) — $15, was $40

The Amazon Echo Dot is about as basic as you can get for a smart home devices, but it’s a good place to start. You communicate with Alexa entirely through voice commands, but that doesn’t limit the voice assistant’s capabilities much. The Echo Dot is also a great addition to your smart home suite if you already have other hubs. Just spread them around the house so you can always reach Alexa. If you need a smart home device with more capabilities, for instance a visual display, there are other Cyber Monday Amazon Echo deals happening this weekend too.

Keurig K-Mini — $50, was $100

If you’re a no-frills coffee drinker, and you like to stick to one cup in the morning, then take advantage of Cyber Monday Keurig deals. Keurig is about the simplest way to get a single cup of coffee, and the K-Mini is the epitome of that. As Keurig is famous for, it only makes one cup at a time, and you have to refill the water reservoir between each brew. You can select several different sizes for your brew, so you can match your favorite coffee mug. This model is ridiculously cheap during the Amazon Cyber Monday deals, so if you’ve ever considered a Keurig, this is your time to try one.

iRobot Roomba 694 — $179, was $274

The Roomba 694 is a great starter robot vacuum. You’ll notice right away how much you don’t miss vacuuming. The 694 is a buy-and-forget type of device. It recharges itself, and uses pattern recognition to figure out the optimal route for cleaning your house. It remembers specific dirty areas and returns there more frequently. It runs great on hard floors and carpet. All you’ll have to do is dump it out every so often. If you want to monitor it even less, you can check out other models in the Cyber Monday Robot Vacuum deals that have features like a home base trash receptor.

Apple AirPods Pro 2 — $200, were $249

AirPods Pro 2 are Apple’s newest and best earbuds. They boast several improvements over the already fantastic AirPods Pro, the biggest of which is in sound quality. They have Apple’s new H2 chip in them, so active noise-cancelling is smarter and stronger than ever before. Apple has slightly adjusted the pressure relief holes on the outside, so they’re a bit more comfortable and you can wear them for longer. It also boosted the battery life, so you get up to six hours of uninterrupted music before needing to use the charging case. There are, of course, several other models of AirPods, but since Cyber Monday AirPods deals are going on right now, why not go for the best?

Apple Watch SE — $229, was $249

The Apple Watch SE is your best option for a mid-price smartwatch. It has most of the capabilities of the regular mainline Apple Watches, including great iPhone connectivity, fitness tracking, and GPS, but at a lower price point. The biggest thing you miss out on is the always-on screen, so you’ll have to let the watch know when you want to check your steps. Some of the other Apple Watch models are on sale during the Cyber Monday Apple Watch deals, but this is one of the cheapest options.

Insignia 50-inch F30 Series 4K TV — $240, was $400

If you just need a bare-bones, cheap TV, Cyber Monday TV deals are the time to buy one. Getting a fairly large 4K TV for under $300 is nothing to turn up your nose at. The Insignia F30 isn’t entirely bare-bones either. It’s equipped with Fire TV, so you can stream all your favorite shows straight to the TV. It can upscale content into 4K, and supports HDR 10 video, so all of your content will look great, from blockbusters to YouTube videos. This is just one of the many Amazon products available in the Amazon Cyber Monday deals.

Apple 10.2-inch iPad (2021) — $270, was $329

The 9th-Gen 10.2-inch iPad is a beautiful tablet, and it’s this cheap only because it’s a few years old now. It has powerful internal components for a tablet, running on the same chip that powered the iPhone 11. It’s screen is the famous Apple Retina display with True Tone, so it looks crisp and produces deep colors. It has an 8MP camera on the back and an even more powerful 12MP front-facing camera, so you’ll look great on the family FaceTimes. A few other models are available in the Cyber Monday iPad deals, so make sure to browse around.

Lenovo IdeaPad Flex 5i Chromebook — $330, was $430

Cyber Monday laptop deals provide a great chance to upgrade your daily computer. There are tons of Lenovo laptops on sale during Cyber Monday Chromebook deals, including some great 2-in-1s. The IdeaPad Flex 5i is the Chromebook version of Lenovo’s average consumer line of laptops. It has the build quality you’d expect from Lenovo, with a comfortable keypad and a solid feel overall. It leans heavier into its tablet strengths than its laptop ones, with a ChromeOS operating system and a Full HD touchscreen. It’s great for students and light computer users who want something flexible and portable.

Sony WH-1000XM5 Headphones — $348, were $399

The Sony WH-1000XM5s are the best headphones on the market today. What else needs to be said? Every year, this line of Sony headphones takes the cake in our rankings. They have great sound quality and fantastic noise-canceling. The newest model has redesigned the top headband, but it’s still comfortable and lightweight. Audiophiles and casual users will both love these headphones, especially since they’re included in the Cyber Monday headphone deals and get a nice price cut.

Asus TUF 15-inch Gaming Laptop — $590, was $650

Scouring Cyber Monday gaming laptop deals so you can upgrade your rig? The Asus TUF 15-inch gaming laptop is a great starting point. It’s not going to blow you out of the water when you check out a modern AAA game, but it will be a huge boost over a standard laptop. It runs on an Intel Core i5 CPU and an Nvidia GeForce GTX 1650 GPU with 8GB of RAM. It has a nice 1080p monitor with an impressive refresh rate of 144Hz. It’s also nice and tough, so it won’t get bricked if it gets knocked around a bit.

Apple Watch Ultra — $739, was $799

The Apple Watch Ultra is the adventurer’s smartwatch. The average user might find some of its features totally pointless, but if you’re the kind of person who takes vacation time to climb mountains, this is the smartwatch for you. It tracks several health metrics at all times, including heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and electrocardiogram. It functions deep underwater, at freezing temperatures, and is virtually immune to scratching. Its GPS system is a huge upgrade over the other Apple Watches, so it’s incredibly accurate.

Apple MacBook Air (2020) — $799, was $999

MacBooks are usually just out of the standard buyer’s price range, so they all get nabbed up during Cyber Monday MacBook deals. This MacBook Air coms with the M1 chip and 8GB of RAM, so it’s fairly powerful. It has an all-day battery life, so it’s great for students and people who travel for work. Amazon Cyber Monday deals feature a few different MacBooks, so if you’re looking for something beefier than the iPad Air, make sure to browse around.

Microsoft Surface Laptop 5 — $1,100, was $1,300

The Microsoft Surface line of tablets brought on a revolution in 2-in-1 laptop technology. The Surface Laptop 5 takes what Microsoft learned with the mainline Surface and brings it back into a standard laptop. It has pretty powerful internal components, with a 12th-Gen Intel Core i7 CPU and the option for 8GB or 16GB of RAM. It’s has a nice and big 15-inch touchscreen with 2496 x 1664 resolution, so it looks great and the touchscreen is accurate. If you’ve ever thought about grabbing a Surface Laptop, don’t sleep on Amazon Cyber Monday deals.
