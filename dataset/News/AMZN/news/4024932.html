Best Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Yoga Ethernet Adapter in 2022

Buying Guides
By Brendan Lowry
last updated 3 June 2022

When you purchase through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission. Here’s how it works.

2-in-1 laptops like the Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Yoga are amazing, but one of their disadvantages is that they're too thin for ethernet ports. This means that you're forced to use Wi-Fi — unless, of course, you get yourself a helpful ethernet adapter. Capable of transferring an ethernet connection through an open USB slot, these adapters are perfect for giving users access to the benefits of wired internet. There are tons of these adapters out there. Still, overall, we feel that the StarTech USB Ethernet Adapter is easily the best one thanks to its excellent and useful features, all while maintaining a reasonably low price.

If you're looking for the best overall ethernet adapter for your Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Yoga, then you can't beat StarTech's state-of-the-art ethernet adapter. It supports 10/100/1000 networks without any issues, but the best part about it is the special tech built within. It's got onboard Checksum, support for Large Send Offload, Jumbo Frames, and Crossover Detection and Auto-Correction. In layman's terms, these features improve connection stability, while also reducing the amount of CPU power that networking will draw on your device, making it perform better and also making its battery last longer, too. The adapter itself is also designed to be lightweight, making it an excellent choice for travelers who are on the move often.

Bottom line

Ethernet adapters are incredible tools that can give you the ability to use ethernet with your convertible laptops, as well as other devices that are too thin for a standard ethernet cable. They're not too expensive, and if you prefer wired internet over wireless, the money you do pay will be very well spent here.

There are tons of different ethernet adapters out there, and each of them offers something that someone will be pleased with. However, when push comes to shove, we feel that the StarTech USB Ethernet Adapter is the undisputed leader of the pack since it offers a ton of helpful and useful features built into it that will improve your overall experience with your device.
