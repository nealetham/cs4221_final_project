Best fake tan for your face - top formulas for 2020

Maintain your golden glow from head to toe. These are the best tanning formulas for your face for a tropical boost, without the sun damage

By
Brijiena Lovelace
  • 18:00, 22 May 2020
  • |
  • comments

With months cooped up indoors, it's safe to say we could all do with some sunshine days to boost our morales and our complexions.

Although we might not get our 4* all inclusive holidays this year, we can still benefit from enviable glow, whatever the weather - yes, even from the stuffy comforts of our homes.

A face specific tanning solution is a great alternative to wearing heavy makeup in the sun, it'll also allow you to reduce overexposure of the sun to your face, whilst keeping your complexion even and boosting hydration.

With self-tanning sales surging and plenty of sun predicted on the way, big beauty retailers such as John Lewis and Feel Unique are slashing prices for all of your DIY tanning jobs.

Below we've rounded up the brands worth trying this summer.

Best fake tan for your face - top formulas for 2020

1. Dove DermaSpa Summer Revived Skin Gradual Fake Tan

Offering a gradual and natural sun-kissed look for fair to medium skin tones, Dove's DermaSpa Summer Revived tan is one to consider if you're looking for an everyday use lotion, that's streak-free and perfect to pull off a post holiday glow.

The formula is lightweight and comprises key moisturising ingredients, compressed from natural seed oils and combined with natural tanners. It helps in preventing moisture loss but, but performs in catering to a healthy tan without the sun exposure.

For £3.50 you can't really go wrong either.

Price: £3.50, Amazon - buy here now

2. Isle of Paradise Self Tanning Drops Medium

Take it from us, tanning drops are the innovation you wished you'd known about sooner, especially when it comes to an even face tan that doesn't run the risk of turning you orange.

The versatile product transforms your favourite products into comfortable and natural looking glow companions. It's available in three tones: light, medium and dark for a streak-free solution to every holiday hue.

Add a few drops to your favourite moisturiser and you and you're pretty much good to go.

Price: £15.95, Feel Unique- buy here now

3. Garnier Ambre Solaire Natural Bronzer Dark Self Tan Face Mist

If you're going dark it's best to go with a super lightweight formula that will allow you to build it up if needed, without jumping straight into the deep end.

Garnier's Ambre Solaire Natural Bronzer features a micro-diffusion nozzle, that sprays an super fine mist for fast drying and even coverage, it even sprays at a slight angle to get all of those harder to reach places easier.

It's enriched with apricot kernel oil for a silky smooth finish to compliment your faux-holiday glow.

Price: £8.20, Amazon - buy here now

4. Lancôme Flash Bronzer Self Tanning Moisturising Face Gel

Lancôme's self tanning face gel reigns supreme among the luxury options out there.

The lightweight formula glides effortlessly across skin and quickly absorbs without a sticky residue. Combing self tanning ingredient DHA, as well as nourishing vitamin E, the tan is natural yet leaves skin looking radiant as though you're fresh off of an island.

Price: £20.25, Debenhams - buy here now

5. Tan-Luxe The Face

Whether you're going for 'warm day in the park' or 'three week island cruise' customise your ideal tan with a few drops of wonder product Tan-Luxe.

It's enriched with skin-loving ingredients like cocoa seed extract and hyaluronic acid for protection and a hydration boost. Pop up to 12 drops of the serum into your moisturiser or other skincare products for the sun-kissed look you desire.

Price: £35, Cult Beauty - buy here now

6. Institut Esthederm Sun Kissed Self-Tanning Face Cream Intense Tan

Looking for that intense and streak-free glow, usually only achieved by cooking in the sun?

Institut Esthederm's soft and intense self tanning range is suitable for all skin types to use for a long lasting and natural look.

Price: £31, SpaceNK - buy here now

7. Vichy Ideal Soleil Face and Body Self-Tan Milk

Achieve a natural tan that begins to progress in as little as an hour.

This self-tanning milk from Vichy offers a radiant complexion for the face that doesn't fade or streak, with nourishing vitamin E among the key active ingredients.

The formula is also enriched with Vichy's soothing thermal spa water.

Price: £10.50, Feel Unique - buy here now

8. Clarins Radiance-Plus Golden Glow Booster

For a glowing and radiant complexion, add a few drops of Clarins Radiance glow booster to your favourite moisturiser, or add as part of your skincare regime.

You'll get a natural looking and even tan that builds gradually and is free from streaks.

Price: £24.30, John Lewis - buy here now

9. The Organic Pharmacy Self Tan

This 100% natural self tanning formula is suitable for vegans and features a host of skin loving ingredients including coconut and sesame oil to nourish skin as well as boost your tan.

Seamlessly blends and absorbs into the skin and provides a pleasant apricot aroma.

Price: £38, The Organic Pharmacy - buy here now

Looking for a goody quality sun protection, for long days lounging in the sun? Check out the best sun creams for your face worth trying now.
