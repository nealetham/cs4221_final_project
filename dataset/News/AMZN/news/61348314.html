Full frame mirrorless cameras have been gaining ground slowly on the more popular DSLR cameras due to their more compact size, improving lenses, and expanding features. Sony Alpha mirrorless cameras are easily the most well-regarded brand and line in this category, with an extensive selection of models and compatible lenses. Big camera brands Canon and Nikon are making headway into the mirrorless market, too, as well as Fujifilm and Panasonic. Photographers who have yet to try mirrorless cameras are recommended to experience the unique nature of this camera type, as it presents many advantages not available on DSLRs.
