                        Quarantine Curation: Where to watch all Marvel Avengers films? Your chronological guide

                        Let's binge-watch the Avengers series. 'cause, why not?

                        Listen to Story

                        Live TV
                        Share
                        Advertisement
                        A poster of Avengers: Endgame.
                        Nairita Mukherjee
                        New Delhi,UPDATED: May 12, 2020 16:18 IST

                        Pop culture has always been known to borrow from real events. Stan Lee's X-Men, introduced in 1963, for example, was a direct product of the Civil Rights Movement of the United States. As the movement was in full swing at the moment (1958-1968), Lee introduced a bunch of mutant teenagers fighting for humans, many of whom actually hated them. Black Panther (1966) and the futuristic African nation of Wakanda is another example, where Lee gave us our first 'black' superhero.

                        advertisement

                        But seldom do we see reality drawing from pop culture, except as we stand at a brink where the entire human race is in danger, with thousands being wiped off in a snap, you wonder if the novel coronavirus took a crash course from Thanos.

                        Too grim, let's roll back. Now, we've all seen that scene from Avengers: Endgame (2019) about 'one round-trip each' now made into a meme about going grocery shopping. The Captain has a plan, but things never go that smoothly, do they? There's always something you weren't prepared for, and then there's always collateral damage - like right now, the delay in the release of Black Widow. So unfair!

                        Initially scheduled for release in May 2020, Black Widow will now break into the scene in November 2020 - that is if things don't change further. And that gives us time to take a quick dip into Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and prepare ourselves for Agent Romanoff. Fortunately, Disney+Hotstar (and Amazon Prime in a couple of cases) gives us the scope to actually binge-watch all the movies like a web series.

                        Bass chronology samajh lijiye. And we can help with that. Here are all the movies you need to watch - and in the right order - to reach Endgame, and then transition into phase four of MCU (any Marvel fan worth their vibranium got that, rest, head to Captain America: The First Avenger on our list). So, excelsior!

                        Phase One:

                        Iron Man (2008)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Tony Stark is Iron Man. Except he isn't the only superhero there is. SHIELD stands for Strategic Hazard Intervention Espionage Logistics Directorate. Also, Nick Fury.

                        The Incredible Hulk (2008)

                        Amazon Prime

                        Need to know:There's no Mark Ruffalo here. Tony Stark is recruiting for the 'team' and guess who he wants? Hint: he's green and incredible!

                        Iron Man 2 (2010)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Enter, Natasha Romanoff. And War Machine. Tony finally figures out how to fix his heart - chucks the electromagnet for an ARC reactor. Something weird has been found in New Mexico.

                        Thor (2011)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:It's Mj¶lnir, which means, the god of thunder Thor is here. He's banished from Asgard and needs to become worthy enough to wield the hammer again. Loki is adopted. Fury has the Tesseract, with the first infinity stone, the Space Stone.

                        Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Steve Rogers aka Captain America, an enhanced soldier, is up after a 70-year sleep. HYDRA and officer Johann Schmidt is misusing the Tesseract. Wakanda and Vibranium, an indestructible metal that Captain's shield is made of, is introduced. Bucky Barnes, remember him. Captain joins the 'team'.

                        The Avengers (2012)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Earth is in danger, Loki is to be blamed, Fury activates the Avengers Initiative. The Avengers come together against the Chitauri fleet, an inter-platanary invader Loki brought in through a wormhole right about New York City. They win and celebrate with shawarma. The second infinity stone, the Mind Stone appears here. So does Thanos.

                        Phase Two:

                        Iron Man 3 (2013)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Tony's hobby, building suits has turned into an obsession. He orders JARVIS to destroy them all in the end for Pepper Potts. Aldrich Killian has developed Extremis and cured his own disability, which means there could be many more enhanced humans like Captain America. Tony surgically removes the ARC and throws it in the sea.

                        Thor: The Dark World (2013)

                        advertisement

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:The Dark Elves, led by Malekith wants the Reality Stone called the Aether - the third infinity stone. Thor and Jane Foster save it and it's taken to Asgard, only to be kept in the safe custody of Taneleer Tivan, the Collector.

                        Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:SHIELD is compromised and is infiltrated by secret HYDRA agents. Bucky is back, now as an enhanced human, and is weaponised against SHIELD. The Twins are introduced.

                        Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know: Peter Quill and Thanos's adopted daughter Gamora are both after the Orb with the Power Stone - the fourth infinity stone. Eventually, it's kept in safe custody in Nova Corp. Groot is alive as baby Groot.

                        Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know: Captain and Romanoff retrieve Loki's sceptre from a secret HYDRA facility. Tony and Bruce discover artificial intelligence in it and decide to power the global defence programme, Ultron, with it. But Ultron mutates and decides the only way to save humanity is to eliminate half of it. Sounds like Thanos? He teams up with the Twins, who later figure out Ultron's evil motives. Thor creates Vision with the Mind Stone to help fight Ultron, Battle of Sokovia happens. Captain's shield isn't the last of the Vibranium. Thanos is coming, his gauntlet is ready.

                        advertisement

                        Ant-Man (2015)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know: Pym particles are introduced, as is the concept of the quantum realm, elements that are going to be crucial later. Scott Lang becomes the new Ant-Man with Hank Pym's suit. Hank's daughter Hope van Dyne becomes the Wasp, like her mother, Janet van Dyne. Janet is lost in the subatomic quantum realm.

                        Phase Three:

                        Captain America: Civil War (2016)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know: Battle of Sokovia makes way for the Sokovian Accord. Wakanda decides not to hide away from the world anymore, but King T'Chaka is killed in the Vienna explosion, believed to be caused by Bucky. It is Avengers vs Avengers, and all for Bucky. Tony learns his parents were killed by a brainwashed Bucky under HYDRA's orders.

                        Doctor Strange (2016)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Doctor Strange studies the astral plane and the other dimensions under a sorcerer, the Ancient One, and through that, we find out that Earth is protected from extraterrestrial threats by a shield created y the three Sanctums in New York, Hong Kong and London. The Time Stone, the fifth infinity stone, appears for the first time. Strange agrees to help Thor find Odin.

                        advertisement

                        Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Quill finds his father, Ego, trusts him only to realise his manipulative powers. Under his control, Quill helps him in his plan to consume the worlds, until he finds out that Ego killed his mother, Meredith. Mantis joins the Guardians, and they destroy Ego's brain. Gamora's sister Nebula is on a quest to kill Thanos.

                        Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

                        Amazon Prime

                        Need to know:Tony and Peter Parker are bonding, and Peter's been drafted into the Avengers. Adrian Toomes is building weapons using Chitauri technology the scavenged from the site after the Battle of New York. Crisis is averted but Peter blows his cover to Toomes, and even declines Tony's offer to join Avengers full-time.

                        Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know: Odin is missing, Thor and Loki team up to find him. Their sister Hela plans to take over Asgard, while Surtur pledges to destroy it, as prophesied in Ragnarok. Thor lands up in planet Sarkaar, ruled by the Grandmaster, to find Hulk, who went missing after the Battle Of Sokovia, as a champion there. The duo escape with 142, a Valkyrie warrior, defeat Hela by letting Asgard get destroyed by Surtur. Ragnarok is basically the rebirth of Asgard, with a few Asgardians settling down on Earth. Loki steals the Tessaract from Asgard's vault.

                        Black Panther (2018)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:After T'Chaka's death in the Vienna explosion, T'Challa is to be crowned king. The Wakandans hunt for stollen Vibranium. N'Jadaka, son of T'Challa's uncle N'Jobu lays claim to the throne. T'Challa is presumably killed in mortal combat with N'Jadaka, but returns to fight him in an epic battle. In the end, T'Challa decides to share Wakandan resources and technology with the world.

                        Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Thor finds the Guardians and are drafted into the Avengers. Thanos is collecting all the infinity stones to execute his diabolical plan. The final, Soul Stone, was procured by sacrificing Gamora. It's Thanos vs fragmented Avengers in different parts of the universe. At the final leg, in Wakanda, Thanos acquires the Mind Stone killing Vision, snaps, and half the population disintegrates into dust. Fury also disappears, right before he could send a signal to one Marvel. Thanos enjoys the sunset, as he always wanted to, once everything was done.

                        Ant-Man and the Wasp (2018)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know: Hank, Scott and Hope team up to rescue Janet from the quantum realm. They build a tunnel to the realm. Janet is rescued and the four continue their work with the subatomic quantum realm and time travel. In the last scene, while Scott is in the tunnel, Janet, Hank and Hope turn to dust, after Thanos's finger snap. Scott is trapped.

                        Captain Marvel (2019)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:Kree is at war with Skrulls, and Vers, rescued and trained by Kree, finds herself in a covert mission, only to land-up on Earth. She meets young Nick Fury and Phil Coulson as agents of SHIELD. Vers finds out she's human, and was officially killed in a crash. She remembers Dr Wendy Lawson, and the crash, and realises Krees hid this information from her and used her as a weapon against the innocent Skrulls. Vers, Fury and others find Lawson's lab and the Tessaract. She destroyed the Kree invasion forcing Ronan the Accuser to retreat. She takes it upon herself to save the universe and flies off. Fury starts planning the Avengers initiative. Goose, the cat, throws up the Tessaract on Fury's desk.

                        Avengers: Endgame (2019)

                        Disney+Hotstar

                        Need to know:The remaining Avengers plan to capture Thanos, get his stones and undo his doing, only to find out he destroyed all the stones. Scott escapes from the subatomic quantum realm and finds half of humanity gone. He approaches the Avengers with a time-travel plan to reverse Thanos's doing. Tony and Bruce build a tunnel, the Avengers divide into teams to go back to specific points in time to fetch the stones before Thanos. Romanoff sacrifices herself to fetch the Soul Stone. It works and all those lost come back to life.

                        Thanos gets a whiff and decides to execute his plan before the scheduled time. An epic battle ensues, with Strange assembling all of the Avengers and Wakandans to fight unitedly against Thanos. Captain Marvel joins in to defend the nine realms against one common enemy. Thanos is defeated, but there's irreversible collateral damage. Tony Stark is dead!

                        Now to Marvel's next, Black Widow. This is Natasha Romanoff's back story, and as fate would have it, we're only getting to know if after she's dead. Or is she?
