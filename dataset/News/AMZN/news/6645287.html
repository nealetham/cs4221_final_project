Kindle Paperwhite, Kindle Are On Sale

22.10.2019 Nate Hoffelder No Comments

Amazon has put the Kindle on sale again this week. The Kindle Paperwhite is selling for $89, and the basic Kindle has been marked down to $64.99.

What’s more, the covers for the Paperwhite and the Kindle are also on sale. The cloth cover for the Kindle was discounted $5, and so was the cloth cover for the Kindle Paperwhite (each is $24.99). the leather cover for the Paperwhite was also marked down $8.
