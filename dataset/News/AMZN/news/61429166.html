Strictly speaking, mirrorless cameras have been available for a long time, since many digital point-and-shoot cameras and even rangefinders lack the reflective mirrors that define the camera type. However, it’s only been in recent years that mirrorless interchangeable-lens cameras (MILC) have gained ground in being a viable alternative for professional photography due to advanced models like the Sony Alpha series or new mirrorless lines from Canon, Nikon, and Fujifilm. With the growing industry seeing more adherents as well as more lenses being developed for them, casual and professional photographers alike will benefit from having a few mirrorless cameras in their arsenal.
