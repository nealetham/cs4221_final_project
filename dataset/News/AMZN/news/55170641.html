Amazon Prime Day 2024: FAQs

When was the first Amazon Prime Day in 2024?

Contrary to the name, Amazon Prime Day actually ran for two days across July 16 and July 17 in 2024. Thousands of deals were available for 48-hours across Tuesday and Wednesday before the sale officially ended at 23:59 on July 17. However, some deals remain available on the days after the sale.

Was there a Prime Day in October 2024?

Amazon ran a second Prime Day-style event on October 8 – 9 in 2024 called Prime Big Deal Days. The retailer ran the event for the first time last year so it has brought it back a second time to kick off the huge end-of-year sales season.

As with the usual Amazon Prime Day sale, the majority fo the deals were only available to Amazon Prime members, so you needed to sign up to get access. This event was a preview of the retailer's big Amazon Black Friday sale, with exclusive record-low prices on some products.

How do I become an Amazon Prime member?

You need to be an Amazon Prime member to access all Prime Day deals. A subscription costs $14.99 per month or $139 for a year.

If you haven't signed up before then new subscribers can get a 30-day Amazon Prime free trial to buy the member-exclusive deals. The free trial also lets you try out all the other membership benefits, including free shipping, access to Prime Video and much more.

You can read our Amazon Prime review for full coverage of what you get as part of a membership. That can help you decide if you want to cancel Amazon Prime once your free trial has ended to avoid being charged the monthly fee.

What are the best Amazon Prime Day deals?

The majority of the best Prime Day deals are on Amazon products The retailer always discounts these products to their lowest prices during Prime Day – even cheaper than we see on Black Friday – which makes it a great time to buy a Fire TV, Kindle, Echo Dot, Fire Tablet and so on.

There are also hundreds of deals in all of the retailer's most popular categories, too, such as TVs, laptops, appliances, smart home tech, gaming, fashion and more. Depending on the product, you can often pay the lowest price that year during Prime Day, though some of the biggest savings are held back for Black Friday in November.
