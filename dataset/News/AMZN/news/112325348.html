What You Missed On Wall Street This Morning
Fly Intel
Jason Keil - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.


1. STOCK NEWS: 

  • Kroger (KR) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q1 [more]
  • Revlon (REV) has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy reorganization [more]
  • The Reserve Bank of India lifted business restrictions imposed on MasterCard (MA) [more]
  • Tesla (TSLA) CEO Elon Musk will reiterate his intentions to purchase Twitter (TWTR) in staff meetings today, The Wall Street Journal says [more]
  • Amazon (AMZN) announced Prime Day will be held on July 12-13 [more]


2. WALL STREET CALLS: 

  • Boeing (BA) upgraded to Buy from Neutral at Citi [more]
  • Sally Beauty (SBH) downgraded to Underweight from Equal Weight at Morgan Stanley [more]
  • Unity Software (U) initiated with a Sell at Benchmark [more]
  • Jefferies analyst Laurence Alexander downgraded five chemical company stocks, saying their risk profile no longer looks attractive in the face of higher interest rates [more]
  • UBS analyst Lloyd Walmsley lowered the firm's price targets on Alphabet (GOOGL), Meta Platforms (META), Pinterest (PINS) and Snap (SNAP) as part of a broader research note on U.S. Online Advertising names [more]


3. AROUND THE WEB: 

  • Hindenburg Research is short Ebix (EBIX), claims company has a "glaring fake revenue problem" [more]
  • Perion Network (PERI) mentioned cautiously by Bear Cave's Dorsey [more]
  • McDonald's (MCD) will pay $1.3B to settle probes in France, Bloomberg reports [more]
  • Abbott (ABT) has halted production at a baby formula plant following storms, WSJ reports [more]
  • Amazon (AMZN) is planning expansion into five new countries, Insider reports [more]


4. MOVERS: 

  • NexTier Oilfield (NEX) gains after providing Q2 and FY22 guidance [more]
  • Acadia (ACAD) higher after Jefferies upgraded the stock to Buy [more]
  • Ginkgo Bioworks (DNA) lowers after Jefferies lowered the firm's price target on the stock [more]
  • Carvana (CVNA) declines after Sam Harland, the partner responsible for Tiger Global Management's bet on the company, is said to have left the hedge fund [more]
  • Warner Bros Discover (WBD) falls after JPMorgan reinstated coverage of the stock with a Neutral rating and $22 price target [more]


5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE: 

  • Jabil (JBL) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q3 [more]
  • Steel Dynamics (STLD) provided upbeat Q2 earnings guidance [more]
  • PROG Holdings (PRG) provided lower-than-expected Q2 and FY22 guidance [more]
  • KLA Corp. (KLAC) reiterated its Q2 earnings and revenue guidance [more]
  • Commercial Metals (CMC) reported upbeat Q3 earnings [more]


INDEXES: 

Near midday, the Dow was down 751.97, or 2.45%, to 29,916.56, the Nasdaq was down 461.83, or 4.16%, to 10,637.32, and the S&P 500 was down 124.49, or 3.28%, to 3,665.50.
