Amazon Launched Their Electric Delivery Van — Here’s Everything You Need To Know

Amazon is rolling out 100,000 electric delivery vans made by Rivian to help fulfill their climate pledge.

Our planet’s climate is evolving, and humans are making it turn for the worst. Corporations like Amazon, the e-commerce giant, are a significant contributor, emitting a whopping 54.17 million metric tons of carbon dioxide and delivering 2.5 billion packages last year. The company must reduce its carbon emissions, and with employees and investors pressuring the company to do more to combat climate change, according to CBS, the company decided to co-found “The Climate Pledge” to reach net-zero by 2040.

And as part of that pledge, Amazon has partnered up with Rivian to unveil its Electric Delivery Vehicle. Business Insider says they’ve ordered 100,000 custom vehicles from the EV maker, with 10,000 of them expected to start delivering packages by 2022 and the rest by 2030. Though it is ten years from now, this new vehicle will notdisappoint.
