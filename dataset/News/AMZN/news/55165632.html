Amazon Prime Day is a great opportunity to find deals on Amazon devices like Fire tablets, Echo, Kindle Paperwhite, Ring Doorbell & Fire TV Stick. With its own brands of products, Amazon has grown to become more than an online shopping portal. Amazon devices grow more popular year by year for their highly desirable combination of innovative design, unique features and great value for the price. For example, the Kindle electronic reader remains the e-reader of choice due to their lightweight and waterproof build and glare-free display. The Kindle Paperwhite and Oasis stand out even more for their impressive battery life, ensuring users can read at any time of the day.

Ring smart home products have expanded after their acquisition by Amazon, bringing improved versions of the Ring Video Doorbell, Ring Alarm, Peephole Cam, and other security cameras. All of these can integrate with Amazon Alexa, making smart speakers such as the Echo, Echo Dot, and Echo Show ideal smart home hubs for families with several compatible Amazon devices.

For home entertainment, the Fire TV Stick and Fire tablets allow family members to play movies, TV shows and other content on either the big screen or on a smaller portable display.

Since it was launched in 2015 to commemorate Amazon’s founding date, the Prime Day sale has always taken place in July. This year, however, Amazon has pushed back Prime Day to much later in the year.
