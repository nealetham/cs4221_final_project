Artificial intelligence is among those technologies that amaze people and business tycoon alike. Previously, AI was a science-fiction theme, but now it is gaining importance in everyday practical life.

Without any doubt, AI has taken the world by storm. No application, machine, and nothing are created that don’t include Artificial Intelligence. Indeed, technology’s most significant gift to humanity is AI.

In the previous year, a significant rise in the adoption of AI was seen around the globe, including various industries. Several business organizations use it to boost their customer experience, enhance their operations, and generate innovations.

Nowadays, various enterprises are not just working to incorporate AI into different forms of technology but are also making a breakthrough for using the same technique in the fields of cybersecurity, health, agriculture, and many more.

But, before that, a brief overview will be provided to inform the readers about how AI is involved in our daily lives. Also, problems that AI aims to solve will be addressed later in the article. How Much Is AI In Our Lives?

Artificial Intelligence is growing on a vast scale. The progressive technology has already acquired significant success in the previous year by reorganizing work procedures in different industries. In the following year, the advancements in AI have shown a steady rise, and apart from the healthcare and banking industry, the technology industry has been influenced the most with AI tools and techniques.

Adobe reportedonly 15% of the organizations were previously using AI at the start of 2018, but more 31% are expected to add until the end of 2019. AI is supposed to boost business productivity by 40%.

Most of the technology-oriented companies are not only building AI solutions but are also utilizing it. Moreover, the tech giants like Apple, IBM, and Google have a habit of attaining smaller AI companies to gain competitive advantage.

Besides chatbots that are mainly used by SMEs, big players have developed their intelligent voice assistants like Google Home, and Microsoft’s Cortana is used to examine human language and give appropriate answers.

This is the time of the fourth industrial revolution with AI, automation, data analytics, deep learning, and MI are revolutionizing every industry while creating vast opportunities for businesses. Companies that are already making use of these technologies have boosted their bottom line and aimed to become better leaders in their respective sectors.

How Is AI Changing Our Daily Lives?

AI is present everywhere and is getting better and better to make our lives easier with innovative technological marvels. Here are different ways by which AI is helping the professionals to change our everyday lives.

AI Increases Productivity

The backbone of any modern business relies on achieving the highest level of productivity. Machine automation produces faster and more effectively while providing all critical information to help the managers to make more informed business decisions.

By using both AI and ML, systems can test hundreds and even thousands of mathematical models of production and outcome possibilities. Moreover, it can be more precise in their analysis while adopting new information such as new product introductions, sudden changes in demands, and supply chain disruptions.

Home Automation And Security

Artificial intelligence is making significant advancement in home automation as digital maids and butlers have already been developed. Home devices from Google and Amazon are also bringing quite a few changes in this sector. Now, you can have home assistants controlling the playlists. What’s more interesting is that you can take these a step further and plug it onto your home security systems.

Enabling facial and voice recognition software through AI innovations is helping in making your home completely burglar and trespasser-proof. The AI-powered home security systems also notify you when children return from school or other whereabouts. Moreover, with facial recognition, it is easy to track down criminals and avoid data breaches.

Facial-recognition technology uses deep learning to learn to match the image on the ID to an individual’s face. The algorithm then looks for specific patterns like basic and complex shapes and returns an output that shows either the image matches with the ID face or not.

Reducing Joblessness and Tediousness At Work

Let’s take the example of chatbots; they cut down a lot of redundant tasks at work for providing better customer service. The real-time simulation for conversations allows chatbots to carry out the same with customers, solving their problems at a quick speed as their human counterparts.

AI has also made it convenient for e-commerce giants such as Amazon automate, whose warehouses are increasing their efficiency and relieving workers of tedious tasks like loading and shipping cargo. When tasks such as fundamental social media interactions happen through AI platforms, it leaves the organizations with some extra time to be productive and make insightful business decisions for the future.

Problems The Use Of AI Aim To Solve

Artificial Intelligence has promised to make our lives by every possible means. Here is a list of issues that AI aims to address in the upcoming time.

Upgrade Privacy Policy

As everything is working towards AI integration, various applications and websites are aiming to upgrade their privacy policy to keep the users aware of the latest changes that are continuously pouring in. Like for instance, to include AI integrated apps, Facebook has decided to assure its users’ complete safety of information while maintaining transparency and upgrading their privacy policy.

AI Chips

AI has now enabled computer chips. An average computer system doesn’t support the AI model; thus, AI-enabled chips are being incorporated separately into the methods to make them function like an artificially intelligent machine. The AI-enabled chips can easily carry out complex mathematical calculations that allow integration of the above mentioned AI trends like facial recognition.

Boost The Cybersecurity Industry

The work of cybersecurity experts is getting more and more complicated all over the globe. It’s not just hackers and other malicious actors who are getting smarter with their attacks, but the potential attack surface is also expanding significantly. All those days are gone when hackers attack a computer. Now, they have cloud services, smartphones, IoT devices, as well as a myriad of other internet-connected gadgets that can be easily compromised and are later used for some other purposes.

For this reason, more and more business organizations are moving towards AI as they are in search for some new tools to help them in combating cybersecurity threats. AI is planning its best to boost the cybersecurity industry. The technology is improving the accuracy of detecting breaches and also the efficiency of cybersecurity analysts. Another tool that can enhance the cybersecurity industry is the use of a VPN. It is also recommended to use a VPN to avoid hackers getting hold of your data. A VPN shields your internet traffic and provides you protection from all the risks of internet privacy.

Conclusion

AI has wiped away many of our daily hurdles, and it aims to get more advanced in the future. In the upcoming, we would be more dependent on AI as there will be more AI-tools to do most of our work.

From family connections to manage the business, the main focus of AI is to increase productivity and make some decisions better and easier.
