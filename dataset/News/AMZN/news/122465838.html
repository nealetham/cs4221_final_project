30 Most Famous Celebrities on Instagram in 2023

Published on September 7, 2023 at 8:16 am by Daniyal Jawaid in Lists, News
Share Tweet Email

In this article, we will list the30 Most Famous Celebrities on Instagram. If you want to skip our analysis of influencer marketing and want to see the top 10most followed celebrities head over to the 10Most Famous Celebritieson Instagram in 2023. 

In 2012, Meta Platforms, Inc. (NASDAQ: META), the owner of Facebook, acquired Instagram for $1 Billion. While many thought of it as a mistake, this move helped solidify Meta’s dominance in social media. In fact, the adoption of Instagram’s features, such as reels, is one of the major reasons for its continued growth. This is evident from Baron Fifth Avenue Growth Fund’s comment about Meta Platforms Inc. (NASDAQ: META) in its Q2, 2023 investor letter:

“Shares of Meta Platforms, Inc. (NASDAQ:META), the world’s largest social network continued their upward trajectory, rising by 35.4% due to stabilizing revenues and ongoing improvements in margins. Meta reported continued growth in Instagram Reels adoption and other new advertising products. In addition, the company’s advancements in AI continue contributing to its targeting and measurement capabilities, while GenAI innovation presents an opportunity for new products and incremental monetization. The company also achieved a significant milestone of 3 billion daily active users across its family of apps, representing a 5% year-over-year increase. User engagement remains robust, with video content and Instagram Reels playing a significant role in user time on the platform. The monetization gap between Reels and other ad formats is steadily narrowing, and Meta anticipates it will reach revenue neutrality by late 2023 or early 2024. Meta has also reported an increase in its speed and agility of execution following the recent organizational changes and cost cuts. Longer term, we believe Meta will benefit from its leadership in mobile advertising, massive user base, innovative culture, leading GenAI research and potential distribution, and technological scale, with further monetization opportunities ahead.”

The photo-sharing application has more than 2 billion monthly active users, which is why it is unsurprising that it is one of themost valuable social media companies in the world.Almost all celebrities and therichest actors in the worldare active on Instagram. With that in mind, one mightwonder who are the most followed celebrities on Instagram, which the second half of this article will uncover. Let’s first see why Instagram has become the go-to place for celebrities and influencers for organic brand building. If you are interested in knowing which countries use Instagram the most, Check out25 Countries with Most Instagram Users in 2023

Some of the key differences between Instagram and Facebook make the former suitable for organic brand building. Instagram is hyper-visual and lets you share photos, videos, stories, and reels with brief captions. Facebook allows for a wide range of content format which includes longer texts, links, videos, articles, and stories. Instagram app has built-in features that help its users create good organic content. Meta Platforms, Inc. (NASDAQ:META)’s Facebook sees Instagram’s content as user-generated content (even if it is by a company) and gives it better organic reach on its platform.

A 2019social media engagement studyreported that brands see a 0.09% engagement rate on their Facebook posts and 1.6% on Instagram. The higher engagement rates have led to celebrities and influencers using Instagram for their brand building. Apparel, footwear, and fashion companies such as Nike, Inc. (NYSE:NKE), Puma, and LVMH understand the appeal of Instagram for their marketing.

Nike, Inc. (NYSE:NKE) utilizes LeBron James, an American professional basketball player and one of themost followed celebritieson Instagram,  for influencer marketing. He frequently shows off his Nike collection on his Instagram. 

Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN)’s Audible is also one brand that can be thought of as extensively using influencer marketing. It is an audiobook platform that lets you stream books on the go. While the majority of the influencers endorse Audible on YouTube, its Instagram page shares endorsements by some of themost followed celebritieson Instagram. One such example is the appearance of Lonnie Rashid Lynn, known by his stage name Common, on Audible’s Instagram page to talk about its celebration of top hip-hop artists. Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) realizes the worth of reach and engagement of influencers. Those with substantial following on social media, can create their Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN)’s influencer account, recommend products, and earn a commission.

Siete Bucks Spirits LLC is co-owned by Dwayne Johnson, who is also known as the Rock and is one of themost followedpeople on Instagram. The company launched a tequila brand “Teremana Liquid” in 2020, which, according to Dwayne Johnson, sold 1 million cases between April 2022 and April 203. The massive success of this brand is owed to Johnson’s following on Instagram. The celebrity actor and former professional wrestler has 389 million followers on Instagram. He frequently posts videos and pictures of enjoying Teremana alone and at parties, which gather significant engagement. One of his recent video posts on Instagram, which shows him enjoying Teremana with a group of friends, has gathered 256,000 likes and 2,682 comments.

With Facebook’s declining organic reach, brands are incentivized to increase their reach through paid ads, which yield better results on Facebook compared to Instagram.Facebook’s ads are also more optimized to appear toward the right people, as opposed to Instagram. Since brands are more likely to invest in paid ads with decreasing organic reach, is it likely that a future algorithm update may decrease Instagram’s organic reach?

Meta givestwo reasonsfor Facebook’s declining organic reach. The first is that with more and more content being produced, there is far more content created than there is time to absorb it. As a result, the competition in the feed is increasing, and it’s getting harder for any story to gain exposure in the feed. Secondly, Meta says that instead of showing all people all the content, the news feed now shows them the content that is more relevant to them to increase engagement. Both of the reasons that Meta cites apply to Instagram. So, it is likely that Instagram’s organic reach may decline. In fact, according to some industry experts, Instagram’s organic reach has already declined by 50% since 2015. However, it is unlikely that Instagram’s organic reach will become less than Facebook’s.
