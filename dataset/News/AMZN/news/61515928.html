One of the questions we get asked by readers, friends, and family around this time of year - and specifically in the weeks close to Black Friday is : should I buy 'X' now or wait for Black Friday itself?

The simple answer now, only 10 days from the big day itself is, almost always yes. This is for a couple of reasons. First is that almost all the big retailers will now honour any further price drops on products, refunding you the difference should it drop further of over the sales period. This is an excellent safety net, and if you can confirm its the case for your PS5 console or accessory, then it means you can safely miss the rush, safe in the knowledge you got what you want and won't be out of pocket.

The second reason is a little more simple and is the fact that many of the prices we'll see now - and particularly from this Friday - will be the Black Friday prices. Period. That's just how retailers roll nowadays with more extended Black Friday price periods rather than really specific deals only on one day.

As a result, we're pretty confident right now - and especially at the end of this week - that the prices we see will be the Black Friday pricing, plus retailers will be honoring any differences should further drops happen.
