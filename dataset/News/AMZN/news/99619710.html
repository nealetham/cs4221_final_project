Fly Intel: Pre-market Movers
Market Mover
Andrew Perez - email
Harley Davidson ascends, Pfizer descends following Q4 reports

Check out this morning's top movers from around Wall Street, compiled by The Fly.


HIGHER -

  • Velodyne Lidar (VLDR) up 38% after disclosing that the company and Amazon (AMZN) entered into a transaction agreement, pursuant to which Velodyne agreed to issue a wholly owned subsidiary of Amazon a warrant to acquire up to 39,594,032 shares of Velodyne's common stock
  • Peloton (PTON) up 1% after announcing Q4 earnings, the departure of CEO John Foley from that role and plans to cut approximately 2,800 global positions


UP AFTER EARNINGS -

  • Harley-Davidson (HOG) up 6%
  • Coty (COTY) up 4%
  • Carrier Global (CARR) up 3%
  • Centene (CNC) up 1%


DOWN AFTER EARNINGS -

  • Pfizer (PFE) down 4%
  • Fiserv (FISV) down 5%
  • KKR (KKR) down 3%
  • Take-Two (TTWO) down 2%


LOWER -

  • General Motors (GM) down 4% after Morgan Stanley analyst Adam Jonas downgraded shares to Equal Weight from Overweight with a price target of $55, down from $75
  • Nvidia (NVDA) down 1% after the company and SoftBank Group (SFTBY) confirmed the termination of the previously announced transaction whereby Nvidia would acquire Arm Limited from SBG
