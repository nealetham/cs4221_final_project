M-tech Eros Plus 4G VoLTE Smartphone Launched for Rs. 4,299

The local smartphone brand M-tech has launched a new entry-level smartphone called as Eros Plus. It is the successor to the earlier launched M-tech Eros smartphone. The smartphone packs a 5-inch FWVGA display and is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core processor. It runs on Android 7.0 Nougat out of the box and offers 4G VoLTE along with other basic connectivity options. There is a 5MP rear camera with LED flash and a VGA front camera. It is backed by a 2,000mAh battery that is rated to give up to 7.5 hours of talk time and lasts up to 240 hours in standby mode.

Reliance Jio Extends Triple Cashback Offer Until December 15th

During the Diwali festive season, the Reliance Jio came with the “Triple Cashback Offer” for Rs. 399 and above recharges. Now, the company further extended the validity of this offer till December 15th. With the triple cashback offer, the users get 100% cashback in the form vouchers of Rs. 50 denominations. There are also Rs. 1,899 worth discount codes for using on e-commerce websites like AJIO, Yatra, and RelianceTrends. The third cashback will be coming from the partnered payment services including Amazon Pay and Paytm.

Canon EOS M100 Mirrorless Camera Launched in India for Rs. 39,995

The EOS M100 is the latest mirrorless camera to launch in India by Canon. It sports a 24.2MP APS-C CMOS sensor with Dual Pixel CMOS AF that can record videos at a maximum of Full HD resolution at 60fps. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi, NFC, and Bluetooth. There is also 3.0-inch LCD touchscreen that can be titled up 180-degrees for capturing self-portrait. The Canon EOS M100 comes in Black color and is priced at Rs. 39,995.

MediaTek Launches MT2621 IoT SoC for Smart Trackers & Wearables

Earlier, we have seen Snapdragon Wear 2100 SoC for wearables from Qualcomm. Today, Taiwan based MediaTek unveiled its first ever IoT processor called MT2621. According to the company, it powers the smart trackers, wearables, and other smaller smart devices. With NB-IoT R14 and GSM/GPRS connectivity, the MT2621 SoC is said to offer extended coverage, cross-market application with network transition function and phone calls. There is a single ARM7 CPU clocked at 260MHz and supports Bluetooth 4.2 for connecting with other nearby devices.

Salora Arya Z4 Smartphone with 5″ Display & 4G VoLTE Launched for Rs 4,499

Salora Arya Z4 is yet another low-cost smartphone from the Indian smartphone brand. It is priced at Rs. 4,499 and will be available in Gold and Black color options. The device comes with a 5-inch display offering FWVGA (854 x 480 pixels) resolution. It includes a quad-core processor coupled with 1GB of RAM and 8GB of internal storage. There is a 5MP rear camera, 2MP front camera, and a 2,400mAh battery. Apart from the one-year manufacturer warranty, Salora is also offering a 120-day replacement with the Arya Z4.
