    Amazon’s first cashless and till-less food shop opens in London today

    Online RETAIL giant Amazon has opened its first completely contactless grocery store in London this morning - and it doesn't even have tills.

    By Bryony Smith
    13:25, Thu, Mar 4, 2021 | UPDATED: 16:07, Thu, Mar 4, 2021
    Link copied Bookmark

    Amazon Alexa: Expert demonstrates three features

    Called Amazon Fresh, shoppers can pick up items and leave without visiting a till. This new futuristic store is based in Ealing, West London, and it’s the first of its kind in Europe. Amazon Fresh is only available to those with an Amazon mobile app. Shoppers are required to scan a QR code on their phone to gain entry to the hi-tec grocery store.

    Once inside the store, customers can pick up any items they want without scanning them.

    Cameras and motion sensors track the shopper and the items chosen from the shelves.

    When the shopper leaves the store, the card associated with their Amazon account isautomatically charged. 

    Customers do not need to visit a till once they have chosen their goods, they can walk straight out.

    Amazon Fresh opened in Ealing, West London (Image: Victoria Jones/PA Wire)

    Shoppers can pick up items and leave without visiting a till (Image: Victoria Jones/PA Wire)

    Whilst members of the public do not need to interact with Amazon workers the company says staff will be happy to answer any queries and will be on site to restock shelves.

    Matt Birch, former Sainsbury's executive who now leads Amazon Fresh Stores UK, said: "The focus was just creating a really easy shopping experience for customers.

    "We recognise that UK customers want to shop in a convenient way so we really think they will appreciate being able to walk in and walk out with the shopping they need.”

    The concept of a contactless grocery store was first launched in the US in 2018.

    Amazon Fresh stores will work in a similar way to the twenty Amazon Go stores the company already runs in the US.

    It’s believed Amazon has plans to open additional Amazon Fresh stores in the UK.

    As many as 30 locations in the UK were being eyed up by the shopping giant.

    The new Amazon Fresh store in Ealing is located at the front of the Ealing Broadway Shopping Centre.

    The supermarket is around 2,500 sq ft and will stock a mixture of own-brand products and well-known brands.

    Customers will also be able to pick up or return other items bought on Amazon.
