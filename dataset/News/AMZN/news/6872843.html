Our favorite affordable robot vacuum is back at its lowest price

Want to transform your floors? This robot vacuum will do the trick.

Credit: Reviewed / Jonathan Chan
Written by Isabelle Kagan, senior editor, commerce expert, and online shopping addict.

Updated July 10, 2019

Products are chosen independently by our editors. Purchases made through our links may earn us a commission.

Stating the obvious here, but one of the worst ways to spend a summer’s day is to be stuck inside cleaning. When the weather is warm and sunny, the absolute last thing I want to think about is how desperately my apartment needs tidying. But alas, the dust bunnies under my couch and the crumbs that I sweep off my bed on a regular basis are not going to pick up themselves. This exact scenario is why a robot vacuum is an actual game changer.

Robot vacuums can be an amazing addition to your cleaning regime and literally transform your floors. But with so many on the market that claim to clean those hard-to-reach areas and leave your floors free of dust and debris, it can be hard to tell which ones will actually do the job well. At Reviewed, we put robot vacuums through a series of rigorous tests in our labs using an obstacle course that is designed to measure their navigation and cleaning abilities, so we know which ones are actually worth your money. One of our top favorites? The Eufy RoboVac 11S.

What's so great about this robot vacuum?

When we put the best robot vacuums to the test, we found that the Eufy RoboVac 11S outperformed almost all others in terms of dirt pickup, quiet operation, and maneuverability. It came in a close second overall, and was named our favorite affordable robot vacuum since it normally costs just under $230 and often goes on sale for $170-$180.

But right now until July 14, you can get the Robovac 11S for just $149 on Amazon, which is a massive $80 dollar discount and matches the lowest price we’ve ever seen for it. All you have to do is clip the $25 coupon on the product page, enter the promo code "ROBOVAC11S" at checkout, and voilà!

The Robovac 11S impressed us mainly because of its powerful suction, which contrasted with how quiet it cleaned. One feature that really stood out was its slim 2.85” profile, which allows it to venture under sofas, beds, and other tough-to-reach areas. As our head honcho of robot vacuum testing, Jonathan Chan, describes it: "One of the major challenges of a robot vacuum is getting your couches and toe kicks. The Eufy 11S stands just under three inches tall, allowing it to hunt down dust bunnies where its thicker competitors can't go."

All in all, if you’re looking for a little help around the house, you’re not likely to find a better-performing robot vacuum at this price point. With Prime Day coming up, there's no guarantee that the price won't drop to a new low, but keep in mind that the 11S is one of the most popular robot vacuums on Amazon, so it may sell out. Whether you decide to buy now or wait for Prime Day, you'll be amazed at just how much dust, hair, and dirt this little guy can suck up. Plus with this sale, it won't leave a huge dent in your wallet.
