If you’d like to stay outdoors for longer even as temperatures drop, then you might want to look into getting patio heaters and fire pits. Both are great outdoor heaters that serve as alternatives to the more expensive in-ground firepit. Patio heaters, in particular, are great for commercial spaces due to their ability to warm larger areas. They’re also low-maintenance and are safe around pets and children.

For those who emphasize ambiance, fire pits add both atmosphere and ambiance with their variety of design options. While they’re not as low-maintenance as patio heaters, fire pits do offer roasting capability, which should come in handy in parties, dinners, and evening barbecues.
