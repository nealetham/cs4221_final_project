Playing arcade and simulation racing games on a controller can be fun, but a better experience can definitely be had with a proper racing wheel. ThrustMaster and Logitech provide some of the best racing wheels in the market for racing aficionados. The ThrustMaster T300 is one of ThrustMaster’s most popular models. It comes with a 1080 degree Force Feedback wheel and three adjustable metal pedals. It’s compatible with the PS3, PS4 and PC. Logitech’s G920 is compatible with the Xbox One, while the Logitech G29 is compatible with the PS3 and PS4. Both are compatible with PC. The only difference between the two, aside from compatibility with certain platforms, are the additional buttons that can be found on the G29. Both come with the racing wheel itself and three pedals.
