It’s the season to celebrate, which means typically, there are parties galore, and having your nails done is a must. But even though this year is a bit different due to Covid-19, that doesn’t mean your nails can’t still look great. So, since going to the salon isn’t ideal right now, we’ve rounded up some of our favorite nail polish kits. These sets are awesome if you’re just shopping for yourself, but also make excellent stocking stuffers for the beauty lovers in your life.

From classic neutral shades to glittery gold, these nail polish sets from brands such as Essie, OPI, Deborah Lippmann, and Butter London are perfect for any occasion. Read on to take a look.

Watch on FN

Keep in mind that Sephora and HSN orders placed after Dec. 11 may not arrive by Christmas day and E.L.F Cosmetics’ standard shipping typically takes six to 10 days. So, make sure to purchase your gifts ASAP!

Butter London Guilty Pleasures Ultimate Lacquer Stash

While on the pricier side, Butter London’s Guilty Pleasures nail polish set delivers two dozen nail lacquers, offering plenty of variety. Each of the four drawers in this colorful case contains six polishes from a different collection, including gel-like creams, shimmering crushed diamond-infused shades, high-shine hues and glittery finishes.

Buy: Butter London Guilty Pleasures… $160

DipWell Dipping Nail Starter Kit

This kit comes with five liquid polishes and three dipping powders for a French or pink and white manicure, plus a buffer, file and cuticle pusher. The application should last three to six weeks without chipping.

Buy: DipWell Dipping Nail Starter Kit $45 (was $50)

INC.redible Cosmic Blur Set

Perfect for wintertime, lovers of the iridescent look are sure to enjoy this Comic Blur nail polish set. In addition to receiving an icy polish formulated with light-catching technology, you also get a versatile highlighter that you can use on your eyelids, cheek and brow line for a shimmery holiday-ready glow.

Buy: INC.redible Cosmic Blur Set $15

For those looking for pedicure perfection, OPI’s primer and gloss set offers the ultimate foundation for a paint job. The formulas ensure up to 11 days of gel-like wear and shine.
