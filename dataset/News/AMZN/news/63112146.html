    With less than 100 days to Christmas, parents and kids alike are beginning to think about what Santa will deliver this year.

    Christmas shopping can be a stress-inducing experience at the best of times, particularly if you're buying for several different age groups.

    Online retailer Amazon have tracked down the hottest toys for 2020 - but you'll have to be quick because these won't be around for long.

    Experts at Amazon have drawn up a list of perfect presents for every age group with old favourites like Barbie, Minecraft, Star Wars, Lego and L.O.L Surprise dolls all making an appearance as well as new favourites like Baby Yoda.

    So, make that Xmas shopping list and check it twice!
