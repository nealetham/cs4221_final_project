Amazon’s ‘Big Style Sale’ is live with deals up to 50% off

  • Updated: Jun. 26, 2020, 2:59 p.m.
  • |Published: Jun. 22, 2020, 3:19 p.m.
By
  • Nicolette Accardi | NJ Advance Media for NJ.com

Amazonmay have postponed its popular Prime Day shopping event due to the coronavirus pandemic, but the online retail giant is now currently holding a different summer sale.

The “Big Style Sale,”a fashion shopping event, is live, and it will last anywhere from seven to 10 days.

Here are the best deals we found. (Note: Amazon says the deals are for a limited-time and can change hourly.)

Accessories

You can find Samsonite and American Tourister luggage for up to 50% off. This two-piece Samsonite luggage set is now $154.99, discounted by 48% from $299.99 and comes in stylish colors like emerald, rose and graphite gray. There’s also Mickey and Minnie Mouse designsfor Disney fans.

For Prime members only, Ray-Ban sunglasses are up to 30% off. You can find the classic Wayfarer and Clubmaster styles, along with other frames. You can also try out select styles for free with Prime Wardrobe.

Watches from the popular brands Timex, Citizen, Tommy Hilfiger, Anne Klein, Michael Kors, Bulova and Fossil areup to 50% off. This Michael Kors Ritz stainless-steel watch is up to 40% off (depending on color).

Select designer Rebecca Minkoff handbags are now up to 50% off. You can also find summery,straw crossbody bags for as low as $11.97.

Shoes

Puma sneakers are up to 30% off. The brand’s iconic suede sneakersare just $25.95 (depending on size and color).

You can find select Steve Madden shoesfor up to 50% off. You can also find Birkenstock-style sandalsfor $13.59.

Select Skechers styles are up to 20% off, along with many variations of New Balance sneakers.

Superga’s stylish platform canvas sneakers are up to 65% off(depending on size and color). Other styles can be foundhere for up to 40% off.

Clothing

You can find select Adidas clothing for up to 30% off. The brand’s classic track jacketis down to $27 (depending on color and size).

Select Levi’s denim is up to 40% off, including jeans, shorts, jackets and overalls.

J.Crew’s clothing under its Merchantile brand can be found forup to 40% off.

You can find deals on tons of NFL, NHL, NBA and NCAA fan gear including sweats, t-shirts, sandal slides, jackets and even bedding.

Amazon is selling these matching sweatshirt and short sets for $33.80.

Face masks

There’s a three-pack of American flag printed face coverings for 15% off, just in time for Independence Day 2020.
