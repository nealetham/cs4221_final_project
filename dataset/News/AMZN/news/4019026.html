Bang & Olufsen debuts waterproof Beoplay EX wireless earbuds

Bang & Olufsen (B&O) has launched its latest wireless earbuds, the $399 Beoplay EX, a model that builds on the technologies found in its previous earbuds by adding greater comfort, larger dynamic drivers, and more protection from dust and water. The Beoplay EX incorporate a set of 9.2 millimeter dynamic drivers -- the largest ever used by B&O in a set of wireless earbuds. They'll produce a "significant upgrade in the power of each audio moment, without compromising on comfort," according to a company press release.
