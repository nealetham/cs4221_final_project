    Absentia: Amazon Prime release trailer for season three

    Absentia is the American thriller drama series starring Castle’s Stana Katic and Gossip Girl’s Patrick Heusinger. Fans have been waiting for more than one year for the new series of Absentia and the show’s release date is fast approaching onAmazon Prime Video.Express.co.ukhas everything you need to know about Absentia.

      How many episodes are in Absentia?

      Absentia season three is coming very soon to Amazon Prime Video.

      The brand new series will premiere on Friday, July 17 on the streaming platform.

      New arrivals on Amazon Prime are usually available to watch from 8am onwards on Friday in the UK.

      However, there is always the possibility Absentia could arrive sooner or later than expected, so fans of the thriller series should keep an eye on the Absentia page on Amazon Prime Video from midnight onwards.

      What will happen in Absentia season 3?

      The third season of Absentia takes place three months after the end of second series, with Emily Byrne (played by Katic) completing her FBI suspension.

      She is also working on becoming a better person and parent to her son Flynn (Patrick McAuley).

      However, Emily’s world is turned upside down when she has to work with her ex-husband Special Agent Nick Durand (Heusinger) on an international criminal case which threatens her and those close to her.

      The synopsis for season three reads: “Following a sequence of deadly events, Emily must race against time to save Nick, but in the process comes to realise there’s a larger conspiracy brewing.

      "Emily’s demand for the FBI to go after Nick is met with resistance. Taking matters into her own hands, she puts a risky plan into action -bringing her face-to-face with the one man who can lead her to Nick, Colin Dawkins (Geoff Bell).

      "Orchestrating a clever ruse along the way with the help of her former FBI instructor, Rowena Kincade (Josette Simon), Emily heads off with Special Agent Cal Isaac (Matthew Le Nevez) inpursuit.

      "It’s a dangerous journey that will take Emily far from Boston, testing her like never before and forcing her to learn to trust again, to love again, and to finally realise her true place in the world after everything she’s been through."

      Who is in the cast of Absentia season 3?

      Katic reprises her role as Special A.gent Emily Byrne alongside Heusinger as Special Agent Nick Durand.

      Also returning is Matthew Le Nevez as Special Agent Cal Isaac alongside Christopher Colquhoun as Special Agent Derek Crown.

      Neil Jackson returns as Jack alongside Matthew le Nevez as Cal, Natasha Little as Special Agent Gunnarsen and Paul Freeman as Warren.

      Joining the cast of Absentia season three is The Level’s Geoff Bell and Broadchurch’s Josette Simon as Colin Dawkins.

      Another newcomer to the cast of Absenta season three is British actress Josette Simons as Rowena Kincade, Emily’s former FBI instructor.
