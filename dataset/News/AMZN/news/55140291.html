Best Google Pixel 4a 5G Screen Protectors 2024

Buying Guides
By Andrew Myrick
Contributions from

 published 8 July 2022

Might be an old phone, but it still needs some protection.

Google launched the Pixel 4a 5G back in 2020. If you invested faith in Google's terrific cameras and a future-ready 5G chip, you're likely to want to secure it with the best Pixel 4a 5G screen protectors. While this phone has been supplanted in the mid-range market, you shouldn't have to part with more dough if something happens to the display. We've rounded up the best Pixel 4a 5G screen protectors you can get to protect the display, along with a few to protect the rear camera module.

These are the best tempered glass screen protectors for the Pixel 4a 5G

Spigen AlignMaster Tempered Glass Screen Protector
Check Amazon

Flawless precision

Spigen's AlignMaster set the trend for other companies to include an alignment tray with their screen protectors. If you are looking for nothing but the best in terms of precision, Spigen has no competition. It is admittedly more expensive than other options, but you get the reliability of a brand that built its reputation with sturdy cases. You get two protectors in the pack, so even if you break one, there's always a backup.

Shizukawill Anti-Glare Screen Protector
Check Amazon

Best for outdoor usage

Brightness can often hamper our ability to read what is on our screens. If you struggle with that problem, this shizukawill anti-glare tempered glass is the perfect remedy. The anti-glare coating creates a matte surface that prevents the glass from reflecting strong lights. Unlike others, the screen protector also wraps around the bezels, guaranteeing maximum protection.

Supershieldz Anti Spy Tempered Glass Screen Protector
View at Amazon

Protecting privacy

All of us have been in situations when we find strangers shamelessly peeping at our phones' screens. If you hate finding yourself stuck like this, get the Supershieldz anti-spy screen protector for your Pixel 4a 5G. It blocks the vision of anybody looking at the display from a slight angle, allowing you to enjoy your private time with your phone. The matte surface also allows you to throw your phone in the same pocket as sharp objects, such as keys, without worrying about scratches.

LK Tempered Glass
Check Amazon

Get some extras

Installing screen protectors can be a daunting task for some, but LK tries to make it easy thanks to the included alignment frame. Along with sporting a cutout for the selfie camera, LK's tempered glass screen protectors are also case-friendly, so you won't have to worry about interference.

Supershieldz Google Pixel 4a (5G) Tempered Glass
Check Amazon

Rounded edges

Supershieldz has been in the screen protector game for years now, and the company makes some of the best protectors you can get. These screen protectors, which come three to a pack, promise to protect your Pixel 4a 5G from scratches, and can help you avoid any bubbles during installation. The hydrophobic and oleophobic coating will also help protect your screen from gross fingerprints or smudges.

OMOTON Bubble Free Tempered Glass (3-pack)
Check Amazon

No bubbles

When you're looking for the best Pixel 4a 5G screen protectors, you'll likely want a purchase that features an installation kit, along with more than just one. This OMOTON screen protector hits all those marks. An alignment frame is included to go along with the three screen protectors in the box.

EGV Tempered Glass Screen Protector (3+3 pack)
Check Amazon

Trusted brand

The EGV kit comes with three tempered glass screen protectors for the front, three more tempered glass protectors for the rear camera, and an alignment kit. The oleophobic coating helps prevent fingerprints, and each protector is designed to be case-friendly, working with any case you want to slap on the Pixel 4a 5G.

UniqueMe Camera Lens Protector (2-pack)
Check Amazon

Just the camera

The Pixel 4a 5G has more than just a glass display. There's always a chance that the camera module glass getting cracked. Thanks to the UniqueMe Camera Lens Protector, you won't have to worry about taking that chance, as this two-pack protects your camera from drops. And there's no need to fret about lens flare from the flash since there's a cutout. Just keep taking those great pictures.

iVoler Tempered Glass Screen Protector
View at Amazon

Unmatched economy

Compared to the options we have listed above, the iVoler glass screen protector for the Pixel 4a 5G offers the best bang for your buck. It comes in a big combo pack, including four screen protectors and two camera lens guards. You also get a tray to position the screen protectors correctly. The company also makes a massive claim of explosion-proof glass, but even if it doesn't stand up to the claim, there are plenty of replacements in the pack.

Lifetime satisfaction

IQ Shield has a unique solution for people who prefer flexible protective films over tempered glass. The company claims this screen protector uses a self-healing property to eradicate scratches. The protector comprises four layers for durability and anti-yellowing properties, and even includes a lifetime replacement warranty.

Great bargain

If you want basic film protection, Supershieldz offers a great bargain with six protective films in a pack for the Pixel 4a 5G. That ensures you can always replace the film if you don't get it right in the first go.

Soliocial Hydrogel Film Screen Protector (pack of 2)
Check Amazon

Ease of application

The TPU protective film for the Pixel 4a 5G is for those with zero experience applying screen protectors. Don't worry if you don't get it right on the first try; the company claims this protector will automatically remove bubbles and heal any scratches. In addition, oil- and water-resistant coatings over the film ensure it doesn't capture sweat smudges easily.

Protect the glass with the best Pixel 4a 5G screen protectors

Some folks don't want to deal with trying to mix and match accessories, and the same is true when finding the best Pixel 4a 5G screen protectors. That's why our favorite pick is the Spigen Screen and Camera protector for the Pixel 4a 5G. This kit comes with two tempered glass screen protectors for the display and for the camera module. For anyone looking for privacy, the Supershieldz anti-spy tempered glass would be another good pick. All of our picks pair well with the best Pixel 4a 5G cases.

For those who would rather have additional screen protectors, and don't care too much about protecting the camera, then we would recommend the iVoler Tempered Glass package. There are six total screen protectors in the box, which should last you for quite a while and give you the liberty a little care-free. Installation of these protectors will be easier than ever with the included alignment frame, so you can get the screen protector placed perfectly.
