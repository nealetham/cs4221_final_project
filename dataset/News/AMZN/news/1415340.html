12 popular patio furniture sets and pieces you can buy online

All you need to never have to feel cooped up indoors again.

Credit: Ruggable / Orahh
Written by Marissa Miller

Updated March 29, 2021

Products are chosen independently by our editors. Purchases made through our links may earn us a commission.

A solid outdoor furniture set is the ideal way to help you escape cabin fever and create some sense of normalcy amid these bizarre times. And until we can safely socialize indoors, backyard hangouts will have to be our go-to mode of gathering.

When shopping for outdoor furniture online, you don't have to feel like you're settling. The best pieces and sets you can buy are sleek, comfortable, durable, and affordable. From full dining sets to chairs, sofas, and more, these 12 top-rated outdoor furniture pieces are all you need to start your summer on the right foot.

Furniture sets

1. A three-piece conversation set

This Amazon best-selling three-piece furniture set with over 1,800 reviews includes two rattan steel-based wicker chairs and a matching end table. Its sponge-filled cushion stands up to the elements and protects against back pain. The tea table measures 15.75 inches wide, which is all the space you need for a couple of colorful drinks (don’t worry about any spills—its tempered glass top makes for easy cleanup). One reviewer—who delighted in its low price for the high quality—shares a helpful hint for setup: “When assembling, don't tighten the screws at first. Put it all together loosely, then tighten them. If you tighten some of them too early, it is hard to get the rest to line up.”

Get Devoko Patio Porch Furniture Set on Amazon for $159.99

2. An eight-piece entertaining set

Whether you’re feeding a crowd or simply craving lots and lots of human attention, this eight-piece acacia wood set with rave reviews on Overstock is a sensible option. It includes two love seats, four armchairs, and two tables, making it ideal for distanced hangouts. Water-resistant, foam-filled cushions are a fan favorite, while customers notice how well they hold up to spills and stains. Pick from one of five colors, from neutral cream to vibrant teal. An overwhelming majority of five-star reviews mention how straightforward it is to put together, and that it’s a great deal given the number of pieces it includes.

Get Christopher Knight Home Carolina Acacia 8-piece Outdoor Sofa Set at Overstock starting at $909.49

3. A three-piece bar-height set

There’s a strong chance you’re using your patio furniture for happy hour. It’s incredible how some height on these two sling chairs and a tempered glass-topped table create the ultimate outdoor bar setting. Of the top-rated patio set, one reviewer says, “We had a hard time finding a 3-piece bistro set with high chairs. We found this one on Walmart's website and the price was fantastic. We've used it the last few weekends with no issues or complaints. I would definitely recommend it to others." Many also note it’s an incredibly durable set given the low price, and the legs are still low enough that you can comfortably dine.

Get the Mainstays Sand Dune 3-Piece Outdoor Bar Height Bistro Set at Walmart for $149.97

Tables

4. A small yet functional bistro set

A space-friendly yet elegant bistro set is the ultimate way to transport yourself to a palm tree-dotted resort without even having to leave your backyard. The armchairs' beachy design is made of woven all-weather cord to resemble rattan and is topped off with plush weather-friendly cushions that'll save your tush during long, lazy afternoons languishing in the sun. The table, topped with a glass surface, is easy to clean and enjoy for daily use (read: no food or drinks seeping through). Given that most bistro tables at this size and quality retail for over $300, you're getting a great deal on the set of armchairs thrown in.

Get the Kuta 3-Pc Chat Set, Light Brown/Navy at One Kings Lane for $334.50

5. A stunning wicker coffee table

Whether you need a place to prop up your feet after a long day or set down an iced latte while you read, a coffee table makes for an ideal centerpiece to your outdoor space. Because it’ll also act as the focal point of your gathering and get lots of traffic, having one with durable materials is essential. This coffee table is made of powder-coated steel to prevent rust and features a faux wood top, an extra bottom ledge for storage, and a brown wicker, water-resistant material. Reviewers consistently note that its wood top is far easier to clean and maintain than the glass-topped variety and that it holds up well to daily use.

Get the Hampton Bay Cambridge Brown Rectangular Wicker Outdoor Patio Coffee Table at The Home Depot for $169

Seating

6. A portable rocking chair

This bring-anywhere rocking chair serves as a comfortable and sturdy place to sit while also keeping your beloved drink safe and sound. It weighs 12 pounds and features a mesh back for breathability. It’s easily foldable, ideal for storing in small spaces once temperatures dip, and perfect for transporting whether you’re moving from the sun to the shade in your yard, or attending a socially distanced get-together. Several of its 2,700 Amazon reviewers love how it features an extra-wide, roomy seat and supports far above the suggested 250-pound limit.

Get the GCI Outdoor Freestyle Rocker Portable Folding Rocking Chair at Amazon for $59.99

7. This lounger with a built-in ottoman

With a perfect rating on Houzz, cozy rustic design, and built-in footrest that seamlessly slides in and out, this wicker and wood lounger is a no-brainer addition to your patio. Reviewers say it's easy to put together with one person and is super comfortable. Given that the lounger is made from eucalyptus hardwood, the brand recommends oiling it with naturally refined linseed oil or teak hardwood oil two to three times per year to retain its weatherproof properties.

Get the Outdoor Interiors Gray Wicker and Eucalyptus Lounger With Built-In Ottoman at Houzz for $279

8. A customizable, modular sectional

What’s incredible about this six-piece sectional is that it operates as a three-in-one seating solution: You can detach each section to form loveseats, lounge chairs, and coffee table or ottoman. It’s outfitted entirely in high-performance fabric that resists discoloration and water damage.

Get the Big Joe Orahh 6-pc. Patio Sectional at Overstock starting at $2,038.99

9. These budget-friendly durable dining chairs

For a chair that’s reliable enough to withstand severe weather, messes, and spills that come with barbecues that last well into the night, these foldable chairs are your best bet. They include drain holes to prevent liquids from pooling, have ergonomic seat and back construction, and come in six sleek colors to match any outdoor setting. With a five-star rating, one reviewer says, “These folding chairs are very sturdy and much more attractive than I anticipated for the price. Although I will use them for extra seating, I actually purchased them to take on a trip. The rental home has a deck but no chairs so I thought these would stack better than the regular folding patio chair. They are perfect."

Get the Lancaster Home Plastic Folding Chairs, Set of 2 at Overstock for $58.99

Decorative items

10. This tropical washable rug

If rugs can tie a room together, they can certainly give your outdoor space a more cohesive look. Not only are they warm and inviting, but they function to protect your feet from dirt, sharp objects, and any dangerous grooves in your cement or wood. Ruggable is the latest and greatest flooring solution to offer completely washable and stylish rugs in virtually every shade and pattern you can think of. They’re made of water-, chlorine-, and UV-resistant polyester and polyurethane, and reviewers say it’s great if you live with “particularly messy beings like pets or kids”: “Bought this rug several months ago. The cat had a messy accident on it yesterday, so I washed it for the first time and it came out good as new. I was really expecting to see stains, but there aren't any. The top was easy to wash and dry.” Want to use it year-round indoors? No problem—simply pair it with a non-slip rug pad of the same size.

Get the Outdoor Filifera Palm Green Rug at Ruggable starting at $159

11. Bright rechargeable lights

Unless you have a fire pit or a flashlight on your phone with an endless battery, those late-nights with good food and good company will require a more sustainable lighting option. These incredibly easy-to-use rechargeable lights illuminate your pathway, yard, or porch for eight hours on a single charge, and come in brown, black, and grey to suit all landscaping styles. Many customers note they emit a light yellow glow that's subtle enough to create a calming ambiance and increase visibility, but not so bright that they’re blinding.

Get Sterno Home Set of 5 Vintage Style Solar Pathway Set at QVC for $45.42

12. A gorgeous woven hammock

Let’s face it: when a swing-hammock-chair hybrid made of woven tapestry also drips of netted fringe and tassels, it doubles as an art piece cut straight from a storybook. This backyard accessory with a near-perfect rating on Overstock supports up to 330 pounds, is suitable for outdoor use, and easily hangs onto branches. Happy customers called out how sturdy and beautiful it is.

Get the Round Tassel Cotton Swing at Overstock for $73.99
