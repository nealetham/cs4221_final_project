    Little Fires Everywhere season 2 release date: Will there be another series?

    LITTLE FIRES EVERYWHERE season 2 is hoped for by fans of the Hulu show. But, has it been confirmed?

    By Fay Watson
    07:20, Thu, May 28, 2020 | UPDATED: 07:23, Thu, May 28, 2020
    Link copied Bookmark

    Little Fires Everywhere: Reese Witherspoon stars in trailer

    Little Fires Everywhere has now been released on Amazon's Prime Video as a new mini-series based on a novel of the same name by Celeste Ng. Originally airing on Hulu in the US earlier this year, some viewers are eager to know whether the Reese Witherspoon-led show could return for another season.

    Trending

      Will there be a Little Fires Everywhere season 2?

      Starring Reese Witherspoon and Kerry Washington as its leads, Little Fires Everywhere was a hit when it was released on Hulu on March 18.

      The show is based on Ng’s 2017 novel and follows the seemingly perfect Richardson family.

      Their lives are upended when a fire wrecks their home before the show flashes back in time to find out how this happened.

      The series starred Witherspoon as journalist and landlord Elena Richardson, whose house is the target of arson.

      While Washington played artist Mia who leads a very different life to Richardson with her teenage daughter.

      The eight-part show was originally billed as a mini-series but some viewers have been wondering if it could return for a second season.

      Hulu has not officially confirmed whether the show will be returning but there is some hope.

      This is because the author of the novel Ng has revealed she is open to writing a sequel.

      Back at a Goodreads Q&A in 2018, a fan asked if she would write a Little Fires Everywhere sequel.

      She replied: “Everything I know about the characters in Little Fires Everywhere is in the book - I promise I'm not holding out on you.

      “But with that said, maybe they'll come back to me for more of their story - never say never.”

      She also said in an interview with Vulture in April: "These characters may come back to me with an idea about more story that needs to be told.

      “So right now I don’t have more to say about them, but maybe three years from now, we’ll make another episode."

      However, speaking to Entertainment Weekly showrunner Liz Tigelaar was not as positive about the possibility.

      She said: "I'd be open to it in terms of this is the best job I've ever had.

      "This was such an amazing treat of work experience, adapting something that I love with all my heart, with people who I love with all my heart."

      However, she did admit she felt the story did suit a mini-series format, adding: "In my heart, I feel like this is what it's always been, which is a limited series.

      “It's a show with a beginning, middle, and an end. Everything burns down."

      "These eight episodes honour the book. This is a close-ended story to me and we've ended it where it ends."

      When will Little Fires Everywhere season 2 be released?

      Given there is no confirmation the show will even return for a second season, there’s no official release date.

      However, if the series planned to wait for a sequel from author Ng then it could be a few years away yet.

      Fans in the UK are still waiting to watch the first season of the mini-series on Amazon’s Prime Video.

      This has now been given a release date of Friday, May 22 on the platform for all eight episodes.

      Express.co.ukwill update this article if anything is revealed about the future of the hit series.

      Little Fires Everywhere launches on Amazon’s Prime Video on May 22.
