What You Missed On Wall Street On Thursday
Fly Intel
Sam Martinelli - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.

1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Walgreens Boots Alliance (WBA) reported better-than-expected Q2 results and reiterated FY22 earnings guidance [more]
  • Amazon (AMZN) renewed its Prime credit card tie-up with JPMorgan's (JPM) Chase unit [more]
  • Tempur Sealy (TPX) expects revenue growth in Q1 of 15%, but warned of sales below expectations following a "strong" Presidents' Day [more]
  • UiPath (PATH) reported upbeat Q4 results but provided "disappointing" guidance for Q1 and FY23 [more]
  • President Biden announced a "historic release" from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve and is authorizing the use of the Defense Production Act for American production of EV materials [more]

2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • AMD (AMD) downgraded to Equal Weight from Overweight at Barclays [more]
  • HP Inc. (HPQ) downgraded to Underweight and Dell Technologies (DELL) downgraded to Equal Weight at Morgan Stanley [more]
  • Citi upgrades Five Below (FIVE) to Buy on "still very long runway for growth" [more]
  • Coinbase (COIN) initiated with a Sell at US Tiger [more]
  • Atlantic Equities starts "priced for perfection" Snowflake (SNOW) with a Neutral rating [more]

3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • Apple (AAPL) has reduced orders to China-based suppliers, DigiTimes reports [more]
  • Qualcomm (QCOM) failed to land Meta (FB) as a flagship AI chip customer, Information reports [more]
  • Ford's (F) Flat Rock Assembly Plant will be down next week due to the chip shortage, Automotive News reports [more]
  • Tesla (TSLA) has extended a production suspension at a Shanghai factory, Reuters reports [more]
  • A Facebook (FB) algorithm mistakenly elevated harmful content, Verge reports [more]

4. MOVERS:

  • Flex (FLEX) gains in New York after reaffirming its Q4 and FY22 EPS and revenue guidance [more]
  • Amylyx (AMLX) falls after announcing the outcome of the FDAâ€™s advisory committee meeting to review the company's New Drug Application for AMX0035 and taurursodiol [more]
  • iQIYI (IQ) and Baidu (BIDU) decline after both companies confirmed they had been identified by the SEC under the Holding Foreign Companies Accountable Act [more]
  • Sharecare (SHCR) and Nano Dimension (NNDM) lower after reporting quarterly results [more]
  • Digital World Acquisition (DWAC) slips after Truth Social downloads sink 95% from launch [more]

5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • Masimo (MASI) reiterated its FY22 revenue guidance, but warned on Q1 revenue [more]
  • Verra Mobility (VRRM) provided upbeat 2022 revenue guidance [more]
  • Nano-X Imaging (NNOX) reported upbeat Q4 results [more]
  • Dare Bioscience (DARE) reported 2021 results and announced its key portfolio objectives [more]
  • Expensify (EXFY) reported what Citi analyst Tyler Radke called a "mixed set" of Q4 results [more]

INDEXES:

The Dow fell 550.46, or 1.56%, to 34,678.35, the Nasdaq lost 221.75, or 1.54%, to 14,220.52, and the S&P 500 declined 72.04, or 1.57%, to 4,530.41.
