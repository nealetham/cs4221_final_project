10 Best pressure washers to buy for incredible outdoor cleaning

Take the pressure out of all the outdoor cleaning. Whether it's the car, the patio or the barbecue accessories, these are the top pressure washers worth investing in now

By
Brijiena Lovelace
  • 19:02, 27 May 2020
  • Updated11:00, 16 Aug 2022
  • |
  • comments

Outdoor cleaning tasks might seem a little bit daunting, if you're armed with a pressure washer however, you'll get maximum results with minimal effort, which is good news all around.

Pressure washers are among one of the more versatile pieces of kit to buy for all of your outdoor needs, effortless cleaning is just around the corner - so we've compiled some information to get you started.

How to choose the best pressure washer:

Choosing the best pressure washer for your needs ultimately comes down to what youll be using it for.

Pressure washers can be used to dust-bust everything from your car, to garden furniture and even grubby patios.

In terms of price range, pressure washers can vary quite drastically, starting anywhere from 70 well up to 400 for some of the more advanced models

What features to look for in a pressure washer:

Size -Pressure washers come in all shapes and sizes, unfortunately, if you end up with one too big t lug around it might mean you use it less than you intend to. Check out the size and weight before rushing to buy a new model.

Pressure (bar) -Pressure is measured in bars. The lower the bar the less pressure you'll have, so ultimately, if you're only planning to use it for small jobs with minimal effort needed, it's best to go for something with lower bars (lower pressure models also tend to be cheaper).

Hose length -The length of the hose you choose to go for will save you a lot of time lugging it around, especially if you have a large surface area to cover. Some nozzles will also have variable pressure settings.

Attachments -The more expensive you go is usually the more attachments that will come with your new pressure washer. Usually washers will just come with a standard lance, sometimes various attachments including dedicated patio cleaners.

Premium options from big retailer Karcher have models ranging from 100 up to a whopping 500, but if you're looking for budget-friendly models, brands like ProKleen are also ranking well at a glance.

If you aren't sure which one is best for you, we've compiled a comprehensive guide of the best pressure washers worth investing in, and which ones will give you the most for your money.

Best pressure washers to buy in the UK

1. Halfords PW10 Pressure Washer

Best value for money pressure washer

For a standard looking but pretty hardworking model that won't cost the earth, the PW10 from Halfords is one well worth considering.

Boasting a powerful 1400W motor and a not too shabby maximum pressure of 105 bar, which is suited to delicates including bicycles, motorbikes, cars and even small patios and decking. It also boasts a lightweight body, meaning carrying it around won't be too much to handle.

An auto stop system ensures no waste, you'll receive a high pressure gun and lance, as well as a 5 metre pressure hose. If you're wanting to cover more ground, 5 metres might not be enough, it does however come with an inlet adaptor and filter for extensions.

Max pressure:105 bar

Hose length: 5m

Weight:4kg

Buy now from Halfords (50).

2. Bosch EasyAquatak 120 High Pressure Washer

Best compact pressure washer

Compared to some of the bulkier models out there, this high pressure washer from Bosch is strikingly compact weighing only 5kg, making all the outdoor cleaning a complete breeze.

The portable cleaner boasts an impressive 1500 power wattage along with a 120 bar performance - good things do come in small packages then.

Thanks to an easy adjustment, the high-pressure detergent nozzle reduces cleaning time by quickly applying your desired soap or cleaning product.

The gadget comes various adjustable nozzles including a rotary nozzle for deep cleaning and a high pressure detergent nozzle which automatically releases the cleaning agent you add, which is perfect for the car or waste bins.

Max pressure:120 bar

Hose length: 5m

Weight:4.7kg

3. ProKleen Electric Pressure Power Washer

Best overall pressure washer

We'd regard this electric pressure washer from ProKleen is one of the best around for under 100.

It's got a sleek and slimline design that won't take up too much storage room and also comes with some nifty features to make cleaning easier, at no extra price.

It's a cold water pressure washer so can't be used with heated temperatures, but what it does have makes it all the more worth it. With a 1600 watt power and 135 bar pressure, the ProKleen can tackle a really grubby car and tidy up the masonry on the house if you need it to.

One of the most ideal features is the carbon brush motor that gives you a flow rate of up to 408L per hour, making light work out of even the grubbiest and most demanding debris on a multitude of surfaces.

Max pressure:135 bar

Hose length:5m

Weight:7kg

4. Karcher K4 Full Control Car and Home Pressure Washer

Best pressure washers for cars

It'd be pretty hard to compile a 'best pressure washer' roundup without featuring a Karcher product.

Leaders in the heavy duty cleaning field, the K4 pressure washer isn't the newest model out, it is however, one of the top rated and on the more affordable side than it's more recent flagships. It's also slightly on the heavier side compared to some of the models already featured, so something to consider if you won't be undertaking big jobs.

The Karcher K4 has an outstanding 1.8kW of power and 130 bar pressure with a flow rate of 420 litres per hour; that's a lot of cleaning power in half the time.

There's a eight metre pressure hose included, which is more than the standard of five. As well as various attachments for different areas of cleaning, there's also detergents and other attachments included in the set too.

Max pressure:130 bar

Hose length: 8m

Weight:11.5kg

Buy now from Kaercher (249.99).

5. Nilfisk Core 140 Power Control

Best award winning pressure washer

The new Nilfisk Core Pressure Washers range combines clever cleaning solutions with striking and powerful features, making it the perfect choice for all types of outdoor domestic cleaning tasks.

A wide range of accessories are also available on the Core collection, so no matter the job, you can be confident the new Pressure Washers can handle it all whether its making sure your car shines or cleaning your garden patio or furniture

Recognised for its simple, clean, and Scandinavian design combined with a striking black frame and blue body, the Nilfisk Core 140 has been awarded the prestigious 2021 German Design Award in the Excellent Product Design, Gardening and Outdoor Living category, as well as the 2021 RED DOT Design award in the Garden category.

Max pressure:140 bar

Hose length:8m

Weight:10kg

6. Auto-Stop FPHPC100 Corded Pressure Washer

Best pressure washer for small tasks

Another cheap and cheerful option worth thinking about for light to medium duty tasks, it's got an impressive 1.4kW of power, but a moderate bar pressure of 100, meaning it won't do all the outdoor tasks with ease, but it will effortlessly clean your car, garden furniture and mild grubby masonry.

It's lightweight in size weighing just under 6kg, perfect for moving around a small patio or for cleaning bikes.

It's got a three metre pressure hose which again, is fitting for all of the lighter tasks that don't require too much of a kick.

Max pressure:100 bar

Hose length:3m

Weight:6kg

7. Karcher K3 Home Pressure Washer

Best pressure washer for patios and decking

If you're okay with an older model, there's nothing wrong with the Karcher K3 and it's got a more desirable price than others it's competing with.

The ergonomic design, including carry handle and wheels is perfect to get around easily, with cables and accessories neatly tucked into place.

It's got 1600 watts of power and only 10 bars less of pressure compared to the K4 model, weighing all things up it's quite the bargain for more than 140 less - if you don't mind not having the newest, it's something worth a think.

It comes with various attachments including a dirt blaster lance, detergent, patio cleaner and much more.

Max pressure:120 bar

Hose length: 6m

Weight:8kg

8. Muc-Off Pressure Washer for Bicycle and Motorcycle Bundle

Best pressure washer for all vehicles

Muc-Off is one of the top rated kits for bikes all around. If you want to give your vehicle the deepest clean, whilst protecting all of it's delicates, this is the set for you.

Featuring a custom-designed snow foam lance, with specific pressure settings for both bicycles and motorcycles without compromising the components.

Included in the kit there's a storage bag, snow foam lance, bike cleaner and litres of the brand's premium bike cleaner - so it's a kit well worth the money instead of buying items individually.

Max pressure:100 bar

Hose length: 3m

Weight:5kg

9. Wolf Blaster Blue 4x4 Pro Electric Pressure Washer

Best heavy duty pressure washer

Boasting an impressive 165 bar of pressure and weighing in at 15kg, the Wolf Blaster pressure washer is the perfect tool to assist you with all your heavy duty and really grimy jobs.

Whether it's blasting tough marks on patio floors or transforming old outdoor furniture to its best condition, this one is fit for everything. However, it's worth noting that with a pump pressure of up to 165, you'll want to adjust this setting for different things if you don't want to erase paint work.

Max pressure:165 bar

Hose length: 6m

Weight:15kg

10. Titan Electric High Pressure Washer

Best pressure washer for power

This heavy duty yet compact design is another contender to watch among the big names in pressure washing.

This model features space-saving integrated accessory storage, making it easier to move around when tackling various jobs.

A quick lock and release system allows you to easily unwind the anti-tangle, 8m high pressure hose.

This pressure washer is suitable for cleaning vehicles, machines, boats and masonry too.

Max pressure:150 bar

Hose length:8m

Weight:13.9kg

Buy now from Ebay (63.99).
