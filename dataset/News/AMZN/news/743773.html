Dedham Planning Board To Hear Amazon Expansion Proposal On June 10

The meeting will be held online via Zoom.

Press Release Desk, News Partner
Posted Fri, Jun 5, 2020 at 5:14 pm ET

Press release from the Town of Dedham:

June 4, 2020

The Planning Board will be holding a virtual "scoping session" at 7 p.m. on Wednesday, June 10, 2020, to hear a proposal from Amazon, which would like to expand its distribution operation based in Dedham.

Find out what's happening in Dedhamwith free, real-time updates from Patch.

The scoping session is a preliminary meeting with the Board, after which Amazon would file an application for Minor Site Plan Review. Amazon is proposing to expand their current operations by utilizing the entire +/- 232,500 sq. ft. building and property located at 480 Sprague Street.

Members of the public can review plans and project documents online.

To attend the virtual meeting on June 10, the Planning Board offers the following way to connect:
