She has a stark warning for parents

    This Christmas, weary parents may have been tempted to do their Yuletide shopping online to beat the queues on the high street.

    But one mum is warning parents to be careful when using Amazon's delivery, saying that it "ruined Christmas" for her young daughter.

    Joanna Lovell ordered a Christmas present for her six-year-old but got a shock when it arrived promptly on the doorstep the next day, according to Hull Live.

    While she praised the quick delivery of the pink and white Barbie Ambulance, she was perplexed because it arrived without any packaging at all - which meant her daughter got an eyeful before she could put it out of sight.

    Joanna said: I'll start by saying I'm a huge fan of Amazon, and actually probably couldn't live without the next-day delivery service.

    I've got Amazon Prime and this month alone I've ordered everything from carpets and door hinges to football socks and a USB card reader from the retail giant.

    I've never had a problem with delivery before, items are always well packaged - if anything they're over-packaged with massive boxes for the smallest of items.

    But I was left in a difficult position with my daughter yesterday after a bright pink and white large Barbie Ambulance - which was meant to be a Christmas present for her, was delivered to our house without any packaging whatsoever.

    When we got home there it was on the doorstep for all to see - and my daughter's eyes nearly popped out of her head with excitement when she saw it.

    I'd had no chance to hide it because although I'd had an alert on the app to say it had been delivered, I never for one moment expected it to arrive as it is, with just an address label stuck to the side.

    She was not warned against this at any point throughout the transaction.

    However, others say Amazon's website clearly states that this item arrive in packaging that reveals whats inside under their Buy Now button.

    The full warning reads: Items arrive in packaging that reveals whats inside. To hide it, choose Ship in Amazon packaging at checkout.

    An Amazon spokesperson said: "Before adding the item to the basket, the website advises:'Item arrives in packaging that reveals what's inside.To hide it, chooseShip in Amazon packagingat checkout.

    "We offer customers the option to conceal their product at no cost by selecting 'Ship in Amazon Packaging' at the checkout.
