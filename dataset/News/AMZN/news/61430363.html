Amazon Australia has offered a sneak peek at its epic Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals across tens of thousands of popular products, including major discounts on big name brands.

Shoppers can expect huge savings on fashion to electronics, toys, home essentials, video games, beauty and more - making it easy to check everyone off the gift list.

With Christmas just five weeks away, the shopping event will offer customers incredible deals over four days no matter what the budget is.
