One way to help reverse bunions is by using what’s called a big toe strap or bunion corrector. Bunions occur when the muscles surrounding the big toe are imbalanced. Specifically, the adductor hallucis is what pulls the big toe closer to your other toes. When you stick your big toes in the loops of the strap and stretch your feet apart, this tool strengthens the opposing muscle, the abductor hallucis, located on the inner arch of the foot. Repeating this exercise can help keep the big toe in its natural position and prevent damage to the toe joint, thus slowing down the progression of bunions. Ahead, we listed a few one-size-fits-all straps for you to try while working, watching tv or lying in bed.
