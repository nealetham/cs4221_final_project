The best Dell laptops come in three main lines: for general work, for home use and for gaming. The flagship XPS line, spearheaded by the XPS 13 and XPS 15 models, are considered among the best Ultrabooks on the market. They feature powerful internals in a chassis and provide users immersive high-resolution displays. The mid-range Inspiron line, on the other hand, gives users a wide range of excellent budget laptops and 2-in-1 convertible options for everyday computing needs. For high-performance gaming, Dell’s Alienware laptops feature powerful graphics processors, fast hard drives and advanced CPUs for every gaming enthusiast.
