Best AirPods deals: Save on AirPods and AirPods Pro

By Andrew Morrisey and Jennifer Allen Updated November 11, 2024

AirPods are regularly among the best Apple deals you can shop, and they also turn up frequently in the best headphone deals taking place. Apple’s popular headphone lineup has several models to choose from, and they compare well to what you’ll find among the current Samsung Galaxy Buds deals and Google Pixel deals. Pretty much every model in the AirPods lineup is seeing a discount right now, and we’ve tracked down all of the savings you can land below. Read onward for those details, and if you aren’t finding the savings you’re looking for have a look at the current Sony headphone deals, Bose headphone deals, or Beats headphones deals as well.

Apple AirPods 2 — $89 $129 31% off

An oldie but a goodie, the Apple AirPods 2 still work well and deliver excellent sound quality. Our Apple AirPods 2 review is nearing its fifth birthday and definitely showing some age, but compliments on these AirPods’ sleek design and solid connection are timeless. For what it’s worth, the people that buy these earbuds — even today — still find them a worthy investment, and they still sport a near-perfect review score over at Best Buy. Tap the button below to check that out for yourself.

Apple AirPods 3 — $94 $169 44% off

The AirPods 3 are the most recent releases of Apple’s entry-level AirPods. They offer substantial upgrades over the AirPods 2 and make a happy medium between the AirPods 2 and the more premium AirPods Pro. The AirPods 3 have spatial audio with dynamic head tracking that makes movies and other content come to life by seemingly placing the sound all around you. An adaptive equalizer allows the headphones to automatically tune music to your ears, and a force sensor lets you easily control your entertainment, answer or end calls, and do even more. These AirPods can reach up to six hours of battery life on a single charge and up to 30 hours of total listening time with the included charging case.

Apple AirPods 4 — $169 $179 6% off

The latest and greatest AirPods are the Apple AirPods 4, and they improve upon previous generations in a number of ways. They come with Active Noise Cancellation and Spatial Audio technologies, and both call quality and sound quality overall are improved. Adaptive Audio seamlessly blends ANC and Transparency mode so you can ensure you hear the world around you while you’re walking or block it out when you’re working. The redesigned case is another upgrade, as it’s smaller than ever and fits easily into most pockets.

Apple AirPods Pro 2 (USB-C) — $190 $249 24% off

Even the latest USB-C AirPods are able to get a sale in. Unless you peer at them closely, they’re quite similar to the old AirPods Pro 2, of course, but there are some fun changes. For example, they’ve added a bit of dust resistance and other audio features, enough so that our AirPods Pro 2 with USB-C review dubbed them the “AirPods Pro 2.5” (which definitely sounds better than the nine-word official title they carry now).

Apple AirPods Max — $459 $549 16% off

With the AirPods Max, not only has Apple made some of the best wireless headphones on the market, but we think they’re the most fun headphones ever. They’re available in a variety of colors and produce some of the best audio found in a pair of wireless headphones. They include active noise-cancelation and Spatial Audio, which makes them a great option for home theater goers, as the Spatial Audio technology creates an immersive experience by tracking your head relative to the audio source. This provides theater-like sound that surrounds you, and lifelike movie and sports experiences. The AirPods Max are incredibly comfortable, as they’re designed with a knit-mesh canopy and memory foam ear cushions. This helps them fit heads of numerous shapes and sizes. The AirPods Max work with any Bluetooth device, but are especially attractive for Apple users, as they can share audio with another set of AirPods, and can switch seamlessly between Apple devices.

How we chose the best AirPods deals

Apple’s lineup of AirPods is popular among headphone shoppers, and it provides a range of options that covers both entry-level listeners and studio pros. Even so, there aren’t that many models in the lineup relatively speaking, and this makes it necessary to keep an eye on all of them when it comes to choosing the best AirPods deals. There are currently four AirPods models on the market and they’re all frequently discounted to various extents. This makes every AirPods model worth including when we talk about the best AirPods deals.

You can find a discount on every AirPods model going on pretty much all of the time, and we keep those prices updated here. The entry-level AirPods 2 are the cheapest AirPods on the market and they’re often made even cheaper with a deal. The newer AirPods 3 and AirPods Pro 2 are also regularly discounted, though it’s not often we find major discounts available. And Apple’s over-ear option, the AirPods Max, are the most expensive of the bunch, which makes them the best candidate for a major discount.

Like all deals, the larger the discount the more it stands out to us. We’re constantly monitoring the best AirPods deals out there, so check back regularly if you’re looking for the most savings you can find. And with each model seeing some sort of discount almost all of the time, you can’t go wrong grabbing some new AirPods whenever you may need them.
