Kass: Now Everybody's Doing It

Here we go, look who's asking about stocks.
  • Author:
    Doug Kass
  • Publish date:
    Aug 6, 2020 1:56 PM EDT
Comments

Birds Do It, Bees Do It... Even Educated Fleas Do It

"When the little blue clock in the middle of his work

Sings a song to the moon up above

It is nature that's all

Simply telling us to fall in love

And that's why birds do it

Bees do it

Even educated fleas do it

Let's do it, let's fall in love"

- Ella Fitzgerald, Let's Do It (Let's Fall in Love)

When Joe Kennedy's shoeshine boy was talking stocks in 1929, Kennedy notoriously sold out his long positions -- saving his fortune from potential losses during The Great Depression.

Kennedy thought the shoeshine boy's interest in stocks was evidence that stock prices were too good to be true.

Well (these are all true) ...

* My Uber driver told me yesterday that he was buying fractional shares in Tesla (TSLA)  and Amazon (AMZN) .

* My accountant's clerk (who makes copies) has a Robinhood account that he trades on 5-10 times a day.

* The guy who cuts my lawn knocked on the door yesterday afternoon and asked what do I think of (MARA)  and (WKHS) ?

* I bought a bagel at my local place yesterday morning. Marty, the owner, told me Nikola (NKLA) is going to win a big contract.

* Last night the assistant pro at my golf club texted me regarding Apple (AAPL)  weekly calls.

* My barber in Palm Beach asked me whether he should pivot out of Amazon and Apple and into value stocks.

* My MD buddy and his wife were over my house on Friday night -- he asked whether he should be buying gold.

They are all doing it:

"In Spain, the best

Upper sets do it

Lithuanians and letts do it

Let's do it, let's fall in love

The dutch in old Amsterdam do it

Not to mention the fins

Folks in Siam do it

Think of Siamese twins

Some Argentines

Without means, do it

People say in Boston even beans do it

Let's do it, let's fall in love

(Amazon and Apple are holdings in Jim Cramer's Action Alerts PLUS member club. Want to be alerted before Jim Cramer buys or sells these stocks? Learn more now.)
