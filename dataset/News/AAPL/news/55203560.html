Apple : iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air are here

October 26, 2020 at 05:55 pm EDT
Share
PHOTOSOctober 23, 2020
iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air are here
On Friday, October 23, iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro with 5G went on sale, ushering in a new era for iPhone. Also making its debut was the all-new iPad Air, featuring the advanced A14 Bionic chip. iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air are now available in stores and arriving in customers' homes around the world.
Customers arrived at Singapore's Apple Orchard Road to buy the new iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air. The vast majority of Apple Store locations around the world are open and operating in various service models to protect the health and well-being of customers and employees.
At Apple's distribution center in Carlisle, Pennsylvania, team members prepared iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air for shipment to stores and customers across the East Coast and Canada.
At UPS Worldport in Louisville, Kentucky, UPS's worldwide air hub, logistics specialists provided support for the global launch of iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air.
Some stores, including Apple Highland Village, are offering Express storefronts, a temporary, convenient new way to pick upApple products. For customers who still wish to shop in stores, Apple team members stock the shelves with the newest product for the first wave of customers to have the best shopping experience.
FedEx made a delivery to an Apple customer in Washington, D.C. In response to COVID-19, Apple's delivery partners are offering contactless delivery, ensuring a safe experience for everyone.
Customers shared their first impressions as they unboxed their new iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air. iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro push the boundaries for users who want the most out of iPhone, with support for an advanced 5G experience, an all-new design with the Ceramic Shield front cover, and a reimagined pro camera system. iPhone 12, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPad Air all feature A14 Bionic, the most advanced Apple-designed chip yet.

Company Profile

Apple Inc. specializes in the design, manufacture and marketing of computer hardware and music supports. Net sales break down by family of products and services as follows: - telephone products (52.1%): iPhone brand; - peripheral devices (10.5%): screens, storage systems, printers, video camera, memory cards, server, switches, etc.; - computers (10.2%): laptops (MacBook, MacBook Air and MacBook Pro brands) and PCs (iMac, Mac mini, Mac Pro and Xserve); - music support (7.4%): music readers iPod and iPad and accessories; - other (19.8%): software, maintenance service and Internet access service, etc. Net sales are distributed geographically as follows: Americas (42.4%), China/Hong Kong/Taiwan (18.9%), Japan (6.3%), Asia/Pacific (7.7%) and Europe/India/Middle East/Africa (24.7%).
