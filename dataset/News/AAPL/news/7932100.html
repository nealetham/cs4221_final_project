    Synopsis

    Tracking the momentum, the index is expected to retest its crucial psychological support level of 8,100 in trade today.

    NEW DELHI: The 50-share Nifty index is expected to open lower on Thursday following muted trend seen in other Asian markets. Tracking the momentum, the index is expected to retest its crucial psychological support level of 8,100 in trade today.

    At 07:30 a.m., Nifty India stock futures in Singapore were trading 31 points lower at 8,114 indicating negative opening on the domestic market.

    Nifty ended the day with a gain of 31 points at a new closing life high of 8,114. The index continued its advance for yet another and has made a new all-time high in trade on Wednesday.

    Intraday support levels are placed at 8070 and 8030 where resistance levels are placed at 8140 - 8170. In the immediate term the level of 7965 is now an important support to watch out for in the next few sessions, say analysts.

    "A breach of the same would induce further profit taking. On the higher side the present upside may now stretch till 8170 to 8235 if we do not witness an immediate reversal from present levels which takes the Nifty down below 7965," GEPL Capital said in a report.

      Overnight, US stocks ended mostly down, as a decline in Apple shares dragged the Nasdaq lower and investors held off on big bets before the European Central Bank's upcoming policy meeting.

      "The European Central Bank will hold a monthly policy meeting on Thursday, where investors will look for clues that the ECB may launch a bond-buying program," Reuters reported.
