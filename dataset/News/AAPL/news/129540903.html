What You Missed on Wall Street On Friday
Mid/Close Wrap
Jason Keil - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.

1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Fed Chair Jerome Powell said in his speech at the Jackson Hole economic symposium that "the time has come for policy to adjust"
  • Workday (WDAY) reported a fiscal Q2 that was slightly better than expected and gave an updated midterm outlook that outlines a pathway to higher profitability and mid-teens growth
  • Intuit (INTU) delivered "a solid Q4" and while there were some puts and takes in the FY25 outlook, this was "largely in line with expectations," according to analysts
  • Cava Group (CAVA) reported what Stifel calls "another solid top-to-bottom beat" in Q2
  • Uber Technologies (UBER) and General Motors' (GM) Cruise announced a multiyear strategic partnership

2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • Guggenheim upgraded Roku (ROKU) to Buy on expected rise in enthusiasm
  • UBS downgraded Las Vegas Sands (LVS) on "protracted" Macau recovery
  • Nomura upgraded Lyft (LYFT) to Neutral on proof of cash generation
  • Raymond James double downgraded Topgolf (MODG) on sales deterioration
  • Peloton (PTON) was downgraded at JPMorgan, here's why

3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • Apple (AAPL) targeting September 20 to start selling new iPhone 16, Bloomberg says
  • UnitedHealth (UNH) and TPG (TPG) among suitors for Surgery Partners (SGRY), Bloomberg says
  • Delta Air Lines' (DAL) Chief Operating Officer Mike Spanos will leave next month to take a role at another company, WSJ says
  • Alibaba (BABA) plans to add a primary listing in Hong Kong by the end of August, FT reports
  • PG&E (PCG) is working to confront an uptick in fires ignited by its power lines, WSJ reports

4. MOVERS:

  • Warby Parker (WRBY) higher after JMP Securities upgraded the stock to Outperform
  • Bilibili (BILI) gains in New York after Barclays upgraded the stock to Overweight
  • Hawaiian Electric (HE) rises after Wells Fargo upgraded the stock to Equal Weight
  • iQiyi (IQ) falls in New York after Benchmark downgraded the stock to Hold
  • Bill (BILL) lower after reporting quarterly results and provided guidance for Q1 and FY25

5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • Hafnia (HAFN) reported Q2 results, with CEO Mikael Skov commenting, "I am delighted to announce that Hafnia in Q2 has once again delivered strong results"
  • Ubiquiti (UI) reported Q4 results, with EPS beating consensus
  • Montana Technologies (AIRJ) reported Q2 results, with EPS higher year-over-year
  • American Software (AMSWA) reported Q1 results and backed its guidance for FY25
  • Red Robin (RRGB) reported Q2 results and provided guidance for FY24

INDEXES:

The Dow rose 462.28, or 1.14%, to 41,175.08, the Nasdaq gained 258.44, or 1.47%, to 17,877.79, and the S&P 500 advanced 63.97, or 1.15%, to 5,634.61.
