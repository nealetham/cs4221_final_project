Man taken to hospital after report of assault in Whitwick

A 48-year-old man has been arrested

    A man has been hospitalised after a report of an assault in Whitwick.

    Residents who live in Whitwick reported seeing a heavy police presence around the area of a former pub called the Crown and Cushion in Silver Street at around 4pm, June 10.

    A resident said two police vans, seven police cars and an ambulance were seen at the location.

    A Leicestershire Police spokesman has now confirmed that they had received a report of an assault and as a result, a 48-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of causing grievous bodily harm.

    Another man, also in his 40s, was found by police and was said to be injured.

    He was taken to hospital to be treated, however, his injuries are not thought to be life-threatening.

    The man arrested remains in custody and officers are continuing to carry out enquiries in the area of Silver Street at this time.

    No other injuries have been reported.
