Apple launches new, more powerful versions of the MacBook Pro

Published: 2024-10-30 11:04:32 ET AAPL

(Reuters) - Apple launched new versions of the MacBook Pro as the company looks to drum up sales of the line of laptops leaning in to demand from professional users who seek more powerful chips for photo, video and audio editing tools.
