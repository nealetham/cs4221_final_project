US trade tribunal denies motion to stay orders in Apple-Masimo dispute

Published: 2023-12-20 21:41:43 ET AAPL

Dec 20 (Reuters) - The U.S. International Trade Commission on Wednesday denied Apple's motion to stay remedial orders pending appeal in its patent dispute with Masimo over technology that enables the blood oxygen feature on some of Apple's smartwatches.

"The Commission has determined to deny Apple's motion to stay the remedial orders pending appeal," it said. (Reporting by Blake Brittain in Washington and Urvi Dugar in Bengaluru; Editing by Matthew Lewis and Christian Schmollinger)
