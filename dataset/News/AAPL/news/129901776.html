September 16th, 2024 12:02
Disney, DirecTV agree to restore programming, B+L mulls sale: Morning Buzz
Mid/Close Wrap
Brianna West - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.

1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Disney (DIS) and DirecTV (T) reached an agreementto restore programming for 11M viewers
  • Alcoa (AA) announced an agreement to sell a 25.1% stake in Ma'aden JV to Ma'aden
  • Demand for Apple's (AAPL) iPhone 16 Pro is lower than expected, according to analyst Ming Chi-Kuo
  • Bausch + Lomb (BLCO) is mulling a sale, FT reports
  • Intel (INTC) reached a $3.5B deal to make chips for the U.S. military, Bloomberg says

2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • Wedbush upgraded Zillow (ZG) to Outperform, raised price target to $80
  • Wells downgraded Colgate (CL) to Underweight with growth normalizing
  • Yelp (YELP) initiated with bearish view at BofA
  • BioNTech (BNTX) was upgraded at JPMorgan and saw its price target raised by $25 at BofA, here's why
  • Ally Financial (ALLY) downgraded to Neutral at BTIG

3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • StoneCo (STNE) is putting Linx up for sale three years after buying the asset, Exame reports
  • Indian antitrust regulator CCI found Amazon (AMZN) and Walmart's (WMT) Flipkart violated local competition laws, Reuters reports
  • Citigroup (C) is shifting responsibility for part of its compliance work and around 800 employees from Chief Operating Officer Anand Selva after the bank was fined $136M by regulators for reporting failures, FT says
  • Boeing (BA) may need to pursue a "mega" equity raise as the way to shore up liquidity while its worker strike drags on, FT suggests
  • Top officials in Berlin were not briefed in advance about an invitation for UniCredit (UNCRY) to bid for a German government stake in Commerzbank (CRZBY), FT reports

4. MOVERS:

  • Nuvalent (NUVL) gains after highlighting the presentation of updated data from the Phase 1 dose-escalation portions of the ongoing ARROS-1 Phase ½ trial of zidesamtinib
  • Ascendis Pharma (ASND) increases in New York after announcing datafrom its ApproaCH trial of TransCon CNP
  • Gevo (GEVO) higher after announcing the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office, PTO, granted a patentfor its ethanol to olefins process
  • iTeos Therapeutics (ITOS) lower after announcing interim datafrom its GALAXIES Lung-201
  • Vizsla Silver (VZLA) falls in New York after announcing a $65M bought deal financing

5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • ImmunoPrecise (IPA) reported Q1 results, with EPS missing consensus
  • ModivCare (MODV) cut its guidancefor FY24
  • Coffee Holding (JVA) reported Q3 results, with EPS higher year-over-year
  • Canoo (GOEV) provided guidance for Septemberand withdrew its previously-issued revenue guidance for FY24
  • Coda Octopus (CODA) announced Q3 results, with CEO Annmarie Gayle commenting, "Management continues to focus on growing the company while continuing to demonstrate that we run a solid and profitable business year-on-year"

INDEXES:

Near midday, the Dow was up 140.93, or 0.34%, to 41,534.71, the Nasdaq was down 153.69, or 0.87%, to 17,530.29, and the S&P 500 was down 8.88, or 0.16%, to 5,617.14.
