2. Best Robo-Adviser: M1 Finance

M1 Finance is a robo-adviser that provides automated investment tools for self-directed investors. With over 100 expert portfolios to choose from, you can create a custom portfolio of stocks and exchange-traded funds (ETFs) or invest in fractional shares. M1 Finance does not charge commissions, trading or management fees; the minimum account balance to start investing is $100. For $95 per year, you can upgrade to M1 Plus and enjoy additional features and benefits. 

M1 Finance supports various types of accounts, including individual and joint taxable accounts, traditional, Roth and simplified employee pension plans (SEP) individual retirement accounts (IRAs) as well as trust and custodial accounts for minors. With M1 Borrow, you can access a flexible line of credit of up to 40% of your portfolio value at a low interest rate. M1 Finance uses a pie-based portfolio system that allows you to allocate your funds proportionately to the slices in your pie. You can create a pie from scratch or add slices of expert pies curated around different goals.

M1 Finance Services

  • Investment management

  • Personal loans
  • Margin loans
  • Banking services

M1 Finance Investment Strategy

M1 Finance provides custom investment portfolios tailored to your specific goals, risk tolerance and time horizon. You have the option to create your portfolio or select one of the expert-designed options. M1 offers micro-investing features such as fractional shares and automatic dividend reinvestment, which help to maximize returns. Dynamic rebalancing algorithms ensure that your portfolio remains aligned with your target weights without any unnecessary trading.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What qualifications should I look for in a financial adviser in Columbus?

A

When choosing a financial adviser in Columbus, look for qualifications such as certified financial planner (CFP) designation and relevant experience in the industry. Also, consider its track record and client reviews for added assurance.

Q

What are some common mistakes to avoid when choosing a financial adviser in Columbus?

A

Avoid common mistakes such as inadequate research on the adviser’s background and qualifications and a lack of understanding of the fees and commissions associated with its services. Steer clear of advisers making unrealistic promises or who pressure you into making decisions you are not comfortable with.

Q

How can a financial adviser in Columbus help me achieve my financial goals?

Afinancial adviser in Columbuscan assist you in developing a tailored financial plan, managing your investments and providing ongoing support to achieve your financial objectives.
