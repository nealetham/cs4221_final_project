What You Missed On Wall Street On Friday
Mid/Close Wrap
Sam Martinelli - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.

1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Dell Technologies (DELL) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q2
  • Marvell (MRVL) reported upbeat Q2 revenue, with CEO Matthew Murphy saying that AI demand has been "extremely strong"
  • Lululemon (LULU) reported mixed Q2 resultsand provided guidance for Q3 and FY24
  • The FDA has granted emergency use authorization for an updated version of Novavax's (NVAX) COVID-19 vaccine
  • Chrysler heir proposes to buyChrysler, Dodge brands from Stellantis (STLA)

2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • Citi upgradedAbercrombie & Fitch (ANF) to Buy with selloff seen bringing opportunity
  • Crowdstrike (CRWD) upgradedto Buy at HSBC
  • Dollar General (DG) downgradedat Morgan Stanley and Telsey Advisory
  • TD Cowen downgradedKohl's (KSS) on challenges in core business
  • Elastic (ESTC) was downgradedat BofA and Baird

3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • Intel (INTC) is considering options, including a foundry split,Bloomberg reports
  • Alibaba (BABA) was praised by China's market regulator as its antitrust review ends, SCMP reports
  • Shell (SHEL) is planning to eliminate roughly 20% of its workforce, Bloomberg reports
  • Nvidia (NVDA) has discussed joining a funding round for OpenAI that would value the AI startup at more than $100B, Bloomberg reports
  • Equinor (EQNR) will downsize its renewables unit, Bloomberg says

4. MOVERS:

  • Intuitive Machines (LUNR) gains after securing a $116.9M NASA Lunar contract award
  • Affirm (AFRM) continues higher after reporting Q2 resultsand providing guidance for FY25 yesterday
  • Roku (ROKU) slips after Lowpass reportedthat Trade Desk (TTD) is building its own smart TV OS
  • Talos Energy (TALO) lower after announcing Tim Duncanhas stepped down as CEO
  • Alnylam (ALNY) falls after announcing results from the HELIOS-B Phase 3 study of vutrisiran

5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • Autodesk (ADSK) reported Q2 resultsand raised its guidance for FY25
  • MongoDB (MDB)reported Q2 results, with CEO Dev Ittycheria commenting, "MongoDB delivered healthy second quarter results"
  • Ulta Beauty (ULTA) reported Q2 results and cut its guidance for FY24
  • JinkoSolar (JKS) announced Q2 results, with EPS beating consensus
  • HashiCorp (HCP) reported Q2 results, with CEO Dave McJannet commenting the "team delivered another solid performance in Q2"

INDEXES:

The Dow rose 228.03, or 0.55%, to 41,563.08, the Nasdaq gained 197.19, or 1.13%, to 17,713.62, and the S&P 500 advanced 56.44, or 1.01%, to 5,648.40.
