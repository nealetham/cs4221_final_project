LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Technavio has been monitoring the wireless display market and it is poised to grow by USD 2.97 billion during 2020-2024, progressing at a CAGR of almost 12% during the forecast period. The report offers an up-to-date analysis regarding the current market scenario, latest trends and drivers, and the overall market environment.

Technavio suggests three forecast scenarios (optimistic, probable, and pessimistic) considering the impact of COVID-19.Please Request Latest Free Sample Report on COVID-19 Impact

The market is fragmented, and the degree of fragmentation will accelerate during the forecast period. Alphabet Inc., Amazon.com Inc., Apple Inc., Intel Corp., Lattice Semiconductor Corp., MediaTek Inc., Microsoft Corp., NETGEAR Inc., Qualcomm Inc., and Roku Inc. are some of the major market participants. To make the most of the opportunities, market vendors should focus more on the growth prospects in the fast-growing segments, while maintaining their positions in the slow-growing segments.

The rising demand for OTT based content has been instrumental in driving the growth of the market. However, interoperability issues associated with wireless displays might hamper the market growth.
