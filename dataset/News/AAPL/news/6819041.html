The doors opened at 11.30am

    Women sporting flowery cocktail dresses and huge hats have descended on Beverley Racecourse today for Ladies' Day.

    Despite heavy rain forecast for later in the day, it hasn't stopped thousands of punters heading down for the races two-and-a-half hours early to soak up the atmosphere of the biggest day in the racecourse's calendar.

    Guests are already in high spirits, with wine, prosecco, beer and cocktails flowing freely. Sniffer dogs were also on hand at the entrance as people queued for the doors to open.
