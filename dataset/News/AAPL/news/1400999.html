Fire, ambulance and police called as car crashes into wall in county road

Emergency services were on the scene

    • Police, the fire service and the ambulance service were on the scene of a crash today.

    The fire service was called at 10.56am to reports of the crash, in which a Range Rover has collided with a wall in Barkby Thorpe Lane, Thurmaston.

    Police statement

    A spokeswoman for Leicestershire Police said:

    We were called at 10.45am to report of an RTC in Barkby Thorpe Lane. It involved two cars, a Range Rover and a Citroen. The ambulance and fire service attended.

    The road was closed from the 'Barkby Thorpe end', towards the junction with Thorpe Lane, end according to police.

    The injuries sustained in the crash are unknown.
