Apple supplier Foxconn's Q3 profit rises 11%, much better than forecasts

Published: 2023-11-14 01:23:27 ET AAPL

TAIPEI, Nov 14 (Reuters) - Apple Inc supplier Foxconn reported on Tuesday a surprise 11% rise in third-quarter profit.

The Taiwanese company, the world's largest contract electronics maker, said net profit for the July-September quarter rose to T$43.1 billion ($1.3 billion) from T$38.8 billion in the same period the previous year.

The profit beat a T$34.5 billion LSEG SmartEstimate, which gives greater weight to forecasts from analysts who are more consistently accurate. ($1 = 32.3430 Taiwan dollars) (Reporting by Yimou Lee and Sarah Wu; Editing by Anne Marie Roantree and Edwina Gibbs)
