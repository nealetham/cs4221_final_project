Wall Street in Fives - Must Read Lists at Midday
Fly Intel
Jason Keil - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these Top Fives, as compiled by the editors of The Fly.


1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Nvidia (NVDA) reported upbeat Q2 results, including an 85% year-over-year boost in gaming revenue [more]
  • Illumina (ILMN) has closed on its deal to acquire Grail for cash, stock and contingent value rights [more]
  • Robinhood (HOOD) reported a wider than expected Q2 loss [more]
  • Cisco (CSCO) reported better than expected Q4 earnings and revenue [more]
  • Macy's (M) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q2 [more]


2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • Nvidia (NVDA) downgraded to Sell from Hold at Summit Insights [more]
  • Oatly Group (OTLY) upgraded to Outperform at RBC Capital on valuation [more]
  • Smartsheet (SMAR) upgraded to Buy from Hold at Jefferies [more]
  • Ontrak (OTRK) downgraded to Hold at Canaccord after customer loss [more]
  • JPMorgan ups Apple (AAPL) price target, sees "multiple catalysts" for shares [more]


3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • Ford (F) will halt production for one week at its Kansas City plant, Reuters reports [more]
  • Toyota (TM) will slash September global production by 40%, Reuters says [more]
  • DoorDash (DASH) block trade offered by SoftBank (SFTBY), Bloomberg reports [more]
  • AbbVie (ABBV) won a court ruling upholding Imbruvica patents, Bloomberg says [more]
  • Amazon (AMZN) intends to open large retail locations in Ohio and California, WSJ reports [more]


4. ï»¿MOVERS:

  • Inovalon (INOV) gains after entering into a definitive agreement to be acquired by an equity consortium led by Nordic Capital for approximately $7.3B [more]
  • GoHealth (GOCO) rises after disclosing in a regulatory filing that its CEO Clinton Jones and Chief Strategy Officer Brandon Cruz each bought 522.3K shares of its stock in two separate transactions of $1.18M [more]
  • DLocal (DLO) and Joyy (YY) higher in New York after reporting quarterly results [more]
  • NRx Pharmaceuticals (NRXP) falls after announcing a $30M private placement [more]
  • Nano-X Imaging (NNOX) declines in New York after disclosing in a regulatory filing the company had received a request for additional information from the FDA concerning its last 510(k) submission of its Nanox.ARC device [more]


5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • Kohl's (KSS) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q2 [more]
  • Victoria's Secret (VSCO) reported Q2 comparable sales rose 5% [more]
  • Bath & Body Works (BBWI) reported Q2 results and provided Q3 earnings guidance [more]
  • Tapestry (TPR) reported upbeat Q4 results [more]
  • Petco (WOOF) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q2 [more]


INDEXES:

Near midday, the Dow was down 47.76, or 0.14%, to 34,912.93, the Nasdaq was up 49.27, or 0.34%, to 14,575.19, and the S&P 500 was up 8.10, or 0.18%, to 4,408.37.
