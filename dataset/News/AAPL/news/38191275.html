    What to expect from Carphone Warehouse's Black Friday 2021?

    Over the last few years, Carphone Warehouse has released early deals ahead of the official Black Friday date.

    It's highly likely the retailer will again choose to launch deals early for 2021, so we'd advise bookmarking product pages and checking the Carphone Warehouse website from the beginning of November.

    Carphone Warehouse Black Friday deals, often referred to as 'Black Tag savings', include a mix of SIM-only deals and price cuts on some of the biggest smartphone models on the market such as the Apple iPhone and Samsung Galaxy.

    SIM-only deals tend to include a range of contracts offered by ID Mobile and Vodafone, with big data bundles a key part of many popular plans.

    With the new iPhone 13 launch on the horizon, iPhone 12 deals are likely to be hot property this year too.

    When is Black Friday 2021?

    This year Black Friday will take place on November 26.

    The date always falls on the day after the US Thanksgiving holiday, which occurs on the fourth Thursday in November.

    Many retailers, like Carphone Warehouse, start launching deals ahead of the official date.

    Ensure you don't miss out on deals as they go live by signing up to newsletters, following retailers on their social channels and checking our Black Friday coverage.

    When is Cyber Monday 2021 and is Carphone Warehouse taking part?

    Cyber Monday falls on November 29 this year.

    Carphone Warehouse typically takes part in Cyber Monday, running discounts from Black Friday through the weekend until midnight on Monday.

    When do Carphone Warehouse Black Friday deals end?

    Usually, Carphone Warehouse Black Friday deals tend to start early and end later than Cyber Monday. We expect the sales to kick in early November and to be up through the first week of December. That being said, the earlier you catch the deals, the better - as the best deals are expected to sell out fast.

    Are Carphone Warehouse phones unlocked?

    The majority of the phones sold by Carphone Warehouse, including nearly all of their SIM-free phones, are unlocked. That means you may use any UK SIM card in your phone and benefit from the services of any UK network.

    Who owns Carphone Warehouse?

    Carphone Warehouse is owned by Dixons Carphone, specialist in electrical and mobile communications.

    Entrepreneurs Sir Charles Dunstone and David Ross launched Carphone Warehouse to capitalise on the boom of mobile phones.

    How much is delivery from Carphone Warehouse?

    Carphone Warehouse offers free next-day delivery to its customers seven days a week via DPD.

    You can find all of the store's shipping info here.

    Does Carphone Warehouse offer discounts?

    Carphone Warehouse does offer discounts to students throughout the year via StudentBeans.

    Unfortunately, the retailer doesn't offer any specific discounts for NHS workers or direct military discounts.
