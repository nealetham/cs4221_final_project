Rewards Bunny Launches Crypto Shopping Reward Mobile App

Published: 2022-03-07 11:34:04 ET AAPL

""People are very busy these days, so we give them an opportunity to earn more by spending less. With Rewards Bunny, people are one easy click away from investment success," says Jacky Goh CEO and Co-Founder of Rewards Bunny."Mobile App is available for download on both Apple and Google Play stores

Singapore -- The creators behind the popular Crypto and USD shopping cashback platform, Rewards Bunny, are pleased to announce that the Rewards Bunny mobile app has launched and is now available to all Apple IOS (NASD: AAPL) and Google Play users. With enhanced data safety and privacy protections, customers can now start shopping online and earning rewards right from their mobile device.

Rewards Bunny offers a wide selection of over 1,000 online stores for your everyday shopping needs, including immensely popular retailers such as Ebay, Walmart (NYSE: WMT), Alibaba and Booking.com, where users can earn your crypto and cashback rewards that multiply like bunnies with the "Earn More" feature. This feature allows the user to multiply rewards already earned by inviting others, checking in, or attaining certain membership tiers.

"People are very busy these days, so we give them an opportunity to earn more by spending less. With Rewards Bunny, people are one easy click away from investment success," says Jacky Goh CEO and Co-Founder of Rewards Bunny. "Many users are hesitant to purchase crypto on designated platforms, so we have designed a system where they can purchase household, as well as luxury items, with their native currency and earn the cashback in crypto. This fosters financial inclusivity in this meteoric rising market and allows the other 99% to participate in the crypto revolution."

About Rewards Bunny

Rewards Bunny is a cashback rewards platform that is making it easy for people to earn, invest in and spend cryptocurrency. When users simply make everyday purchases they have the opportunity to receive their rewards in either cryptocurrency or USD. Rewards Bunny management are experts in marketing technology, game development, graphic design, blockchain, and providing a wonderful experience to start their crypto journey. The company plans to expand its platform with NFT's and to offer payment solutions with a Crypto debit card.
