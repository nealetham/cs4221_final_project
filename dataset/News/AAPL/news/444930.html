      Here’s How Apple and Google Plan to Track the Coronavirus Through Your Phone

      The idea requires widespread adoption and broad testing of potentially infected people; it isn’t clear whether government and public-health officials will get behind it

      By

      Sam Schechner

      and

      Rolfe Winkler

      Do Apple Inc. AAPL 0.32%increase; green up pointing triangle and Alphabet Inc.’s GOOGL -1.20%decrease; red down pointing triangle Google hold the key for tracking the spread of Covid-19 and possibly reopening the global economy?

      The tech giants on Friday said they will release tools for software developers to create so-called contact-tracing apps that record when smartphones come into close contact with each other. Such apps could warn users if they were nearby someone later diagnosed as positive for Covid-19.
