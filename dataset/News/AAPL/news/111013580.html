What You Missed On Wall Street On Tuesday
Fly Intel
Sam Martinelli - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.

1. STOCK NEWS:
  • Walmart (WMT) reported lower-than-expected earnings and cut its FY23 profit guidance [more]
  • Home Depot (HD) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q1 [more]
  • Berkshire Hathaway (BRK.A) bought shares of Citi (C) and Paramount Global (PARA) in Q1 [more]
  • Tesla (TSLA) founder Elon Musk said his takeover of Twitter (TWTR) "cannot move forward" until Twitter CEO Parag Agrawal proves claims on fake accounts [more]
  • Voya Financial (VOYA) and Allianz (ALIZY) announced plans to enter into a long-term strategic partnership [more]
2. WALL STREET CALLS:
  • AMD (AMD) upgraded to Overweight from Neutral at Piper Sandler [more]
  • Workday (WDAY) downgraded to Neutral from Buy at UBS ahead of upcoming Q1 report [more]
  • Shake Shack (SHAK) upgraded to Market Perform at Raymond James [more]
  • Molson Coors (TAP) downgraded to Market Perform from Outperform at Bernstein [more]
  • Maxar Technologies (MAXR) downgraded to Underperform from Neutral at BofA [more]
3. AROUND THE WEB:
  • Q2 Holdings (QTWO) is exploring a sale after receiving interest, Bloomberg reports [more]
  • Spruce Point said it is shorting Alpha Metallurgical (AMR) [more]
  • Lordstown's (RIDE) ability to stay in business remains in doubt, WSJ reports [more]
  • Apple (AAPL) delayed a plan to have employees in the office three days a week, Bloomberg reports [more]
  • Flight data indicates that someone intentionally crashed a China Eastern (CEA) flight on a Boeing (BA) 737-800 earlier this year, WSJ reports [more]
4. MOVERS:
  • Nano Dimension (NNDM) higher in New York after initiating an up to $100M share repurchase plan process [more]
  • IonQ (IONQ) gains after reporting quarterly results and providing Q2 and FY22 guidance [more]
  • Jumia Technologies (JMIA) rose in New York trading after reporting quarterly results [more]
  • Annaly Capital (NLY) declines after announcing a 100M share offering [more]
  • Nerdy (NRDY) lower after reporting quarterly results and providing Q2 and FY22 guidance [more]
5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:
  • Take-Two (TTWO) reported downbeat Q4 results and provided lower-than-expected FY23 net bookings guidance [more]
  • Heska (HSKA) provided revenue guidance for 2023 and 2024 [more]
  • JD.com (JD) reported upbeat Q1 results [more]
  • United Airlines (UAL) raised its Q2 TRASM outlook [more]
  • Bird Global (BRDS) provided FY22 revenue guidance and said it is taking action to accelerate its path to profitability [more]
INDEXES:
The Dow rose 431.17, or 1.34%, to 32,654.59, the Nasdaq gained 321.73, or 2.76%, to 11,984.52, and the S&P 500 advanced 80.84, or 2.02%, to 4,088.85.
