    Apple stock falls ahead of earnings on China lockdown concerns

    Yahoo Finance Video
    Mon, Apr 25, 2022, 10:38 PM

    In This Article:

    AAPL
    -0.12%

    Yahoo Finance Live’s Brian Sozzi discusses the dip in Apple stock as the potential of an expanded COVID-19 lockdown in China creates concerns over the company's production.

    Video Transcript

    BRIAN SOZZI: All right, before we head to break, check out shares of Apple, as Bob was just talking about. The stock is under pressure on fears lockdowns in China could derail production of iPhones and various other Apple products. Shares are down about 3 and 1/2% in the past five days ahead of the company's April 28 earnings release, where pros say the company could announce a major new share buyback plan.
