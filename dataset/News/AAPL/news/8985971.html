Burst water main causes floods as South Ockendon residents are evacuated

A number of residents have been led to safety

essexlive
  • Bookmark
  • Share
By
Kirstie McCrumNetwork Content Editor
  • Updated19:32, 20 JUN 2020
  • Bookmark

    • Firefighters are currently at the scene of a burst water main in South Ockendon.

    Crews have reported that around 50 houses have been affected by the flooding.

    A number of residents were led to safety in the early stages, but there are no reported injuries and crews are supporting all residents and keeping them informed of progress.

    The water has also caused the road to lift.

    Road closures are in place and the incident is expected to last for some time.

    Essex Fire Service tweeted: "We're currently at Cullen Square in South Ockendon after a water main bust and flooded the road.

    "We're working with Essex and Suffolk Water to bring it under control.

    "Please avoid the area while we do this."
