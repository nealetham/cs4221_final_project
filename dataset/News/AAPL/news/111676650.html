What You Missed On Wall Street On Friday
Fly Intel
Sam Martinelli - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these five Top Five lists.

1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Lululemon (LULU) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q1 [more]
  • Bristol-Myers (BMY) agreed to acquire Turning Point Therapeutics (TPTX) for $4.1B in equity value or $76 per share [more]
  • Frontier Airlines (ULCC) and Spirit (SAVE) announced an amended merger agreement [more]
  • GM's (GM) Cruise was granted a driverless AV Passenger Service Deployment permit in California [more]
  • Kohl's (KSS) received bids from Sycamore Partners and Franchise Group, WSJ says [more]

2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • Micron (MU) downgraded to Underweight from Neutral at Piper Sandler [more]
  • Apple's (AAPL) weaker May App Store growth poses risk to Q3 estimate, says Morgan Stanley [more]
  • Ecolab (ECL) upgraded to Outperform from Neutral at Credit Suisse [more]
  • Travelers (TRV) downgraded to Sell from Neutral at Goldman Sachs [more]
  • Snowflake (SNOW) initiated with an Outperform at Raymond James [more]

3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • Tesla (TSLA) will pause hiring as Elon Musk wants to cut 10% of jobs, Reuters reports [more]
  • A Kohl's deal could take weeks or longer, CNBC reports [more]
  • Revlon (REV) is in talks with lenders to push out debt maturities, WSJ reports [more]
  • Apple (AAPL) has agreed to make work schedules at its retail stores more flexible, Bloomberg reports [more]
  • Boeing (BA) paused 737 MAX production for about 10 days in May, WSJ reports [more]

4. MOVERS:

  • Amylyx (AMLX) surges after announcing an FDA extension of its review timeline for the new drug application for AMX0035 [more]
  • Chegg (CHGG) rises after increasing its share repurchase program by $1B [more]
  • StoneCo (STNE) higher after reporting quarterly results [more]
  • Novavax (NVAX) drops after the FDA noted myocarditis events in vaccine review documents [more]
  • Zumiez (ZUMZ) lower after reporting quarterly results [more]

5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • RH (RH) reported upbeat Q1 results and authorized an additional $2B in share repurchases [more]
  • Asana (ASAN) reported upbeat Q1 earnings and revenue [more]
  • CrowdStrike (CRWD) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q1 [more]
  • Joann (JOAN) reported downbeat Q1 results [more]
  • Okta (OKTA) also issued a "beat and raise" report for Q1 [more]

INDEXES:

The Dow fell 348.58, or 1.05%, to 32,899.70, the Nasdaq lost 304.16, or 2.47%, to 12,012.73, and the S&P 500 declined 68.28, or 1.63%, to 4,108.54.
