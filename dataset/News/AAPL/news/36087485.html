One of the most awaited grand sale of the year the Amazon Freedom Sale is now live in India. An array of latest and high-tech mobile phones are up for grabs at a much discounted price.
The Freedom Sale 2020 started at midnight today. The four-day sale is going to have a bunch of launches and deals across a lot of tech products, including but not limited to, smartphones, accessories, headphones, IoT devices, and a lot more. If you’re aware of how Freedom Sale usually work,s you know there is barely any time to look for and understand the top deals out there.
So if you are looking to replace your old smartphone with a new one, you might consider some of these exciting offers:
  • The super stylish OnePlus 7T Pro is now available on Amazon at a discount of Rs 10,000
  • The stylish and top notch Apple iPhone 11 is available at just Rs 62,900 ; at a discount of 8%

  • The Samsung Galaxy M31 is available at a starting price of Rs 17,499

  • OnePlus 8Pro 5G is available at starting price of Rs 54,999

  • The price of the Redmi 8A Dual has come down to just Rs 7,499

  • Up to 25% discount on the Vivo Y91i the sale price of which is merely Rs 7,990

  • Save Rs 3,000 on the Samsung Galaxy A31, which is currently available at a discount of 13%

  • Honor 9X with the pop-up camera feature can be purchased for Rs 15,999

  • The Apple iPhone 11 Pro Max is up for grabs at Rs 1,17,099

  • Price of OPPO A12 has also come down to Rs 9,990

  • Samsung Galaxy M21 is also available in sale, the price of which has come down to Rs 13,999

Here is a brief about our top picks:
The latest entrant in the iPhone series, the Apple iPhone 11 is available at an interesting offer in the ongoing Amazon Prime Day sale. The sale price of the smartphone has come down to Rs 62,900 from its regular market price of Rs 5,400.
A new dual‑camera system of iPhone 11captures more of what you see and love. The fastest chip ever in a smartphone and all‑day battery life let you do more and charge less.
If you are a loyal OnePlus smartphone lover then this may be the right time for you to to get your hands on the latest OnePlus 8 handset. The 6GB RAM + 128GB Storage variant of the mobile phone is available at Rs 41,999 in the ongoing Amazon Prime Day sale.
The mobile phone is available in Glacial Green, Interstellar Glow and Onyx Black colours and in 12GB RAM + 256GB, 6GB RAM + 128GB and 8GB RAM + 128GB Storage variants.
The OPPO Reno4 Pro is available at a discounted price of Rs 34,990. The smartphone is available at a discount of 8% and you can save Rs 3,000 on purchasing it now from Amazon. With the exchange offer available on the handset you can save an extra Rs 11,500.
The smooth and immersive experience of the 90Hz High Refresh Rate Screen of the new
OPPO Reno4 Pro is what makes it so impressive.
Honor recently launched the 9X in India with a full-screen display and a pop-up camera. It features a 48MP triple camera setup on the back and a 4,000mAh battery. The device during the sale is priced at Rs 15,999.
The powerful and highly responsive Kirin 710F octa-core processor of the smartphoneensures all of your favourite games and apps run smoothly with great power efficiency.
