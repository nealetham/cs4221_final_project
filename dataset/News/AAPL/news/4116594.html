Health Kick Podcast: The science behind 2019’s hottest IPO and the company’s plans for 2020

Health & Biotech

December 11, 2019February 12, 2020 | Tim Boreham

share

clipboard

Stockhead’s resident Health and Biotech expert Tim Boreham is putting down the microscope and picking up the microphone for another instalment of The Health Kick Podcast.

In this episode Tim chats with Osteopore (ASX:OSX) executive director Geoff Pocock.

Osteopore is a developer of bioresorbable bone healing implants that dissolve over time, leaving only healthy bone tissue.

Made using 3D techniques, Osteopore’s implants are a natural alternative to bone grafts or metallic implants such as titanium.

The company had a monumental debut on the ASX, jumping a whopping 300 per cent above its 63c IPO price.

So tune in to hear the pair discuss Osteopore’s game changing tech, deals in China and plans for 2020.
