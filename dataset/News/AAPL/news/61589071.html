Manchester United's inconsistency and Giroud's fab four – Football Weekly Extra

Presented byMax RushdenwithBarry Glendenning,Jonathan Liewand Marcus Bean. Produced by Joel Grove
Thu 3 Dec 2020 15.16 GMTLast modified on Thu 3 Dec 2020 15.17 GMT
Share
00:00:0000:00:00

Max Rushden is joined by Barry Glendenning, Jonathan Liew and Marcus Bean to discuss United, Oliver Giroud and question if Pablo Picasso is French. Plus; Archie Rhind-Tutt joins us for all things German football

Rate, review, share onApple Podcasts,Soundcloud,Audioboom,Mixcloud,AcastandStitcher, and join the conversation onFacebook,Twitterandemail.

PSG beat Manchester United 3-1 at Old Trafford, leaving their Champions League group with Leipzig very much in the balance, but how much of the blame can be sent Ole Gunnar Solskjær’s way?

Elsewhere, we heap deserved praise on Olivier Giroud after a four-goal haul against Sevilla, while in other goal news, Cristiano Ronaldo nets the 750th of his career.

Plus: Archie joins us from Cologne for all things German football (plus a minute on Fulham), fans are back in English grounds and we preview this weekend’s Premier League games, including a hotly-anticipated north London derby.

See you on Monday.
