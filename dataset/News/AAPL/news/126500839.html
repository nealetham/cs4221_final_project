Apple buys startup DarwinAI, adds staff to its AI division, Bloomberg reports

Published: 2024-03-14 13:25:07 ET AAPL

March 14 (Reuters) - Apple has bought Canadian artificial intelligence (AI) startup DarwinAI and added dozens of the company's staffers to its AI division, Bloomberg News reported on Thursday. (Reporting by Arsheeya Bajwa in Bengaluru; Editing by Pooja Desai)
