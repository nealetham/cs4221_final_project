Apple’s new M1 MacBook Pro is a great and powerful gift for the holidays, but unlike the Wizard of Oz, there are no tricks involved in its magic. It is based on the sort of technological wizardry that Apple has been using to wow the industry lately. The 13-inch MacBook Pro will put a smile on the face of anyone that needs a laptop, even tech fans that already have a computer.

✕ Remove Ads

The newest MacBook Pro was announced in November of 2020, along with the Mac mini and MacBook Air. These three computers are the first of a new generation based on Apple Silicon. What that means is the M1 chip that handles central and graphics processing is quite similar to the chip used in the iPhone 12 and iPad Air. This allows the new Mac computers to run apps that were designed for Apple’s mobile devices. Of course, it can execute Mac apps as well.

Related: Why An Apple Watch Is The Perfect Holiday Gift For An iPhone Owner

The latest 13-inch MacBook Pro is a fantastic idea for anyone shopping for that big gift for someone special. It’s high-quality, fast, efficient, and a pleasure to use. Apple gifts are always welcome, it being among the most popular brands globally. The screen is bright at 500 nits and features True Tone technology for whites that match the ambient lighting. For color space, it is hard to beat its P3 color that displays 25 percent more colors than standard sRGB screens. Plus, an external monitor can be connected supporting up to 6K resolution at 60Hz. The sound output delivers Dolby Atmos playback also, so this is quite a nice gift for movie and music fans as well.

✕ Remove Ads

Why Apple's M1 Is Special

The M1 system-on-a-chip that powers the new 13-inch MacBook Pro is not only super-fast but is also incredibly efficient. Compared to the previous generation that was released earlier in the year, the M1 MacBook Pro is up to 2.8-times faster with as much as 5-times better graphics performance. The M1 also includes a Neural Engine for up to 11-times faster machine learning. For all of this raw power, the battery life is the longest of any Mac ever made, giving up to 20 hours of use. That’s incredible and has been put to the test by third-party reviewers and found to be true.

The MacBook Pro is actually powerful enough to replace a desktop computer. For good posture, a laptop stand can lift the screen higher and a Bluetooth keyboard will make this a double-duty gift, replacing an older desktop as well as making a super-powerful laptop replacement.
