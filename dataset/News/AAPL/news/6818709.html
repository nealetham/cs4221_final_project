10 pictures that perfectly sum up Beverley Races Ladies Day 2019

Thousands once again descended on Beverley Westwood for a wet but wonderful Ladies Day

  • Bookmark
  • Share
  • Comments
What's On
By
Alex GroveSenior reporter
  • 21:05, 14 AUG 2019
  • Bookmark

    • It may not have been the warmest or driest Ladies Day at Beverley Racecourse but spirits were far from dampened as thousands of race-goers hit Beverley Westwood on Wednesday.

    Despite downpours, people dressed to impress on another incredibly well attended day at the races and there were plenty of familiar sights along the way.

    Our photographer snapped dozens of you enjoying your day out in Beverley and judging by the pictures you all had an amazing time.

    The sun may have been missing but there was plenty of laughter with many of you enjoying a flutter and having a tipple to truly indulge in everything that Ladies Day has to offer.

    We could have chosen so many more but here are 10 photos which we think best sum up Beverley Races Ladies Day 2019 in all its glory.
