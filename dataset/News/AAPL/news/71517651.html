Wall Street in Fives - Must Read Lists at Midday
Fly Intel
Jason Keil - email
Five top five lists with the news and calls moving stocks, powered by The Fly.

Get caught up quickly on the top news and calls moving stocks with these Top Fives, as compiled by the editors of The Fly.


1. STOCK NEWS:

  • Johnson & Johnson (JNJ) provided a "beat and raise" report for Q3 and said it plans to file for full approval of its COVID-19 vaccine [more]
  • Travelers (TRV) reported better than expected Q3 core earnings and revenue [more]
  • Procter & Gamble (PG) reiterated FY22 earnings and revenue growth guidance [more]
  • Atea (AVIR) plunged after its Phase 2 MOONSONG trial of a potential COVID-19 treatment did not meet its primary endpoint [more]
  • Steel Dynamics (STLD) reported upbeat Q3 results [more]


2. WALL STREET CALLS:

  • Walmart (WMT) upgraded to Conviction Buy from Buy at Goldman Sachs [more]
  • Target (TGT) downgraded to Buy from Conviction Buy at Goldman Sachs [more]
  • Albemarle (ALB) upgraded to Outperform from Sector Perform at RBC Capital [more]
  • JPMorgan sees Q4 beat for Apple (AAPL) with "deja-vu" guidance for Q1 [more]
  • Wedbush downgrades Zillow Group (ZG) to Neutral on Zillow Offers purchases pause [more]


3. AROUND THE WEB:

  • The SEC's GameStop (GME) report reviews issues with "game-like" brokerage apps, WSJ reports [more]
  • Information on alleged collusion between Facebook (FB) and Google (GOOGL) will go public, NY Post reports [more]
  • Microsoft's (MSFT) Xbox boss Phil Spencer said that Xbox Game Studios is "definitely not done" with acquisitions, The Verge says [more]
  • Credit Suisse (CS) is in talks with the DOJ to settle a probe into its role in a Mozambique bond scandal, Bloomberg reports [more]
  • The FDA may act this week on mix-and-match for COVID-19 vaccines, NY Times says [more]


4. MOVERS:

  • Energous (WATT) rises after gaining FCC approval for its Active Energy Harvesting transmitter technology [more]
  • Merck (MRK) higher after Atea's (AVIR) COVID drug missed its target in a Phase 2 study [more]
  • Galera Therapeutics (GRTX) plunges after its Phase 3 ROMAN trial did not meet its primary endpoint [more]
  • dLocal (DLO) slips after filing to sell 16M shares of common stock [more]
  • TaskUs (TASK) lower after filing to sell 10M shares of common stock [more]


5. EARNINGS/GUIDANCE:

  • EverQuote (EVER) lowered its Q3 revenue guidance [more]
  • Philip Morris (PM) reported upbeat Q3 results [more]
  • Halliburton (HAL) reported better than expected Q3 adjusted earnings [more]
  • Kansas City Southern (KSU) reported downbeat Q3 earnings [more]
  • ManpowerGroup (MAN) provided Q4 earnings guidance [more]


INDEXES:

Near midday, the Dow was up 177.74, or 0.50%, to 35,436.35, the Nasdaq was up 99.74, or 0.66%, to 15,121.55, and the S&P 500 was up 30.28, or 0.67%, to 4,516.74.
