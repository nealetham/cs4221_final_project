The best Apple Watch deals right now

BySam BlumUpdated July 14, 2020 2:06 p.m.

Throughout a day, you could try to organize your life the old-fashioned way—or you could use an Apple Watch. If you’re on the hunt for a bargain, the Apple Watch is never out of reach, given the bevy of affordable options and financing plans available. We’ve scoured the internet for the very best deals and supplied them for you here, so you can get back to optimizing your life, instead of fussing about how to optimize your life.

Best Apple Watch deals right now

Tech products are always evolving, which is why there’s now five different Apple Watch series on the market. Everyone needs a device that fits their specific needs, so feel free to peruse the various specs for each device, which Apple has kindly provided. Pro tip: The price range is a rather big one, with the oldest available models starting at $169 and increasing upwards of $529.

Still, the price you’re going to pay is all incumbent upon your needs and whatever specs you deem essential.

Apple Watch Series 3 (GPS, 38mm)—was $199 now $169 at Walmart 
Apple Watch Nike+ Series 4 (GPS, 44mm)—$246 at Best Buy 
Apple Watch Series 3 (GPS + Cellular, 38mm)—was $379 now $299 at Walmart 
Apple Watch Series 5 (GPS, 40mm)—was $400 now $380 on Amazon
Apple Watch Series 5 (GPS + Cellular, 44mm)—$529 at BestBuy

It might seem like a major outlay for a watch, but the purchase doesn’t need to be made in one lump sum. There are a variety of financing deals available, so you can lease the Apple Watch on slower, more affordable terms. The $299 Apple Series 3 Watch, for example, can be yours through Affirm’s financing plan via payments of $28 a month.

Additionally, older generation Apple Watches come at lower prices than those detailed above, albeit without the newer generation specs. Walmart is currently peddling a refurbished Series 2 (GPS, 42mm) for a more modest $144.49.
