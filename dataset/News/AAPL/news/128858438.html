                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Lenovo to put AI into all PCs by 2027: device chief

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Chinese and U.S. consumers showing 'a lot of interest,' Luca Rossi says

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Lenovo Group looks to boost sales with AI-powered PC after seeing demand drop off from the pandemic. (Photo by Tomoko Wakasugi)

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          SHUNSUKE TABETA, Nikkei staff writer
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          July 24, 2024 02:21 JST

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          BEIJING -- Lenovo Group, the world's top supplier of personal computers, seeks to equip all its new PCs with artificial intelligence features by 2027, a top executive said.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          "Every PC will have some level of AI," predicted Luca Rossi, the head of the company's Intelligence Devices Group, in an interview with Nikkei. The group oversees PCs and smartphones.
