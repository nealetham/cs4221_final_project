New Laundry Service Explodes into Roscoe Village

Chicago's #1 Dry Cleaner launches subscription laundry service

Neighborhood News, Neighbor
Posted Fri, May 22, 2020 at 1:35 pm CT

CD One Pickup and Delivery has officially rolled into Roscoe Village and the surrounding areas of Lakeview, Wrigleyville, Uptown, and Lincoln Park. The 36 unit Chicago-based retail chain will now pick up, wash, fold, and delivery laundry the very next day for under $21 per week – all you have to do is place your clothes, sheets, and towels in the free tote provided to you and CD One will do the rest! Savings can even be found on dry cleaning as laundered and pressed shirts are just $1.99 and any garment dry cleaned is just $4.99. And what's even better is that Patch.com readers get $20 off when they sign up with code PATCH.

"We are eager to introduce CD One Pickup and Delivery to the community and positively impact the lifestyles of our customers by eliminating laundry from their life," said Jonathon Reckles, Director of Marketing for CD One Price Cleaners. "A good number of our customers use shared laundry rooms or laundromats. There are so many great things our customers will be able to do with their free time now that they have fewer chores to worry about – binge watching the latest shows, extra opportunities for hobbies or even something as simple as reading a book. We are thrilled to be able to give these communities the chance to regain some of life's simple pleasures."

Affordable and easy to use, the subscription laundry service is designed for those who would prefer to be doing something other than spending countless hours sorting, washing, drying and folding. With a variety of package options, customers can choose how often they want their laundry or dry cleaning done – weekly, every other week, or just a quick one-time pick up. Households can then fill their provided tote with as much laundry as they can fit – around 25 pounds – and place it outside for pickup on their scheduled laundry day in order to get it back the very next day. Subscribers will also have access to mobile-friendly features on the official CD One Pickup and Delivery mobile app, available on both Apple and Google Play stores.

In addition, customers are welcome to include any dry-cleaning garments and shirts to be laundered and pressed for $4.99 and $1.99 per piece respectively in a separately provided bag. Regardless of the plan, CD One guarantees two things: your laundry will be cleaned, folded and delivered the very next day, and it will always be done the right way – backed by the CD One Pickup and Delivery Guarantee. Residents in the area can use promo code PATCH to get $20 off their first month's subscription.


The operational and customer service expertise is a reflection of more than three decades of experience with sister brand CD One Price Cleaners. The well-known industry leader and one of the most reputable dry-cleaning providers in the Midwest serves as the foundation for the new pickup and delivery service. Rafiq Karimi Jr., CEO of CD One Price Cleaners and the architect behind CD One Pickup and Delivery, has grown the dry-cleaning company out of the Chicagoland area since 2001 and now has 36 franchise and corporate owned locations throughout Illinois, Minnesota, Indiana and Missouri.

"Our team of clothing care professionals has already reinvented the dry-cleaning experience for modern day customers and now we are doing the same for laundry," said Karimi. "After 35 years in the industry, it is clear that this is where laundry is heading. Today's families and generations to come will benefit from the added free time and reduced responsibility that CD One provides."

About CD One Pickup and Delivery
CD One Pickup and Delivery is the trusted laundry and dry cleaning pick-up and delivery expert for laundry loathing households. With affordable subscriptions and next-day delivery, CD One always gets laundry done the right way, helping families look good and feel good while putting free time back into their hands. CD One laundry specialists have more than 35 years in the clothing care industry and the company stands behind its service with its CD One Pickup and Delivery Guarantee
