Stockhead’s ‘Garimpeiro’ columnist Barry FitzGerald is back in the studio for another instalment of The Explorers Podcast.

In this episode Barry chats to Matthew Carr, executive director of Titan Minerals (ASX:TTM).

Titan Minerals is an Andean gold and copper explorer focused on exploration and definition of high-grade gold resources in Ecuador and Peru.
