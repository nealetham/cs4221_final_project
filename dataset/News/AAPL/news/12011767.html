Come Dine With Me are looking for Hull couples for the next series

It usually takes two to tango

    They say too many cooks spoil the broth - but that's exactly what top TV producers are looking for.

    It may cause arguments along the way, but working together like a well-oiled machine to create something to send the tastebuds into overdrive is always a worthy bounty.

    And if that's not the best part, you could also get £1,000 if your plate is rated the best because Couples Come Dine With Me is on the lookout for couples in Hull and East Yorkshire to take part in its next series.

    Despite temperatures usually rising in the kitchen, if you think you can handle the pressure then get in contact with producers to put you and your loved one's skills to the test.

    Not only will you get to put your joint cooking skills to the test, you'll also get to spend two nights dining out at fellow contestants' homes and be in with the chance of winning £1,000.

    The casting call says: "Over three days of filming, three couples, from all walks of life, take turns to host the perfect dinner party.

    "At the end of the week the best couple wins £1,000."

    How to apply

    You and your partner will need to be available for filming (and cooking) between Tuesday July, 9 and Friday July, 12.

    If these dates are convenient, all you need to do is fill out an online application form.

    The form is simple and asks you to provide basic details, plus your Twitter, Facebook and Instagram usernames, a small paragraph about you and your partner and the option to upload a non-compulsory picture of you both.
