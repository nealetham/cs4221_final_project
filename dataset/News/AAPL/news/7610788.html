You might be surprised to find out how long you can keep running on empty

    When you see the orange light on your dashboard start flickering panic sets in and you head to the nearest petrol station, right?

    Well, not so fast, as Money Supermarket claim that for the majority of cars, you still have an average of 40 miles left in the tank before you're completely out of juice.

    In a Ford Fiesta, the most common car type in the country, you have 37 miles left after the petrol tank comes on. For the rest of the ten most common car types, you have the following amount of miles left; Vauxhall Corse (29 miles), Ford Focus (40), Volkswagen Golf (42), Vauxhall Astra (26), Volkswagen Polo (39), Audi A3 (42), Mercedes-Benz C Class (46), MINI Cooper (45).

    The other most common car type, the Nissan Qashqai, has no available statistics- so act promptly when the petrol light comes on in this case.

    Is it dangerous to drive with the low fuel light on?

    Of course, it isn't dangerous to drive with the low fuel light on- but it does pose an unnecessary and easily avoidable risk.

    If the low fuel light does flash on, use your satnav or Google Maps to direct you to the nearest petrol station.
